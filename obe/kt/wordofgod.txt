====== word of God, word of Yahweh, word of the Lord, scripture ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "word of God" refers to everything that God has communicated to people. This includes spoken and written messages.

  * Other terms in the Bible that have the same meaning are, "the word of Yahweh" and "the word of the Lord."
  * The term "scriptures" refers to the written message that God told people to write. Many years after God's words were written down, people can still read these scriptures and know God's teachings.
  * In the New Testament, Jesus is called "the Word" and "the Word of God." These titles mean that Jesus fully reveals who God is. He can do this because he is God himself.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context, other ways to translate this term could include, "the message of Yahweh" or "God's message" or "the teachings from God."
  * It may be more natural in some languages to make this term plural and say "God's words" or "the words of Yahweh."
  * The expression "the word of God (or Yahweh) came" is often used to introduce something that God told his prophet or his people. This could be translated as, "Yahweh spoke these words" or "God spoke this message."
  * The term "scripture" or "scriptures" could be translated as "the writings" or "the written message from God."

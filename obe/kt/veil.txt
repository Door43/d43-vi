====== veil ======

===== Definition: =====

A veil is a head covering worn mainly by women in OT times to conceal, protect or enhance the face. As a verb, to veil is to cover with, hide or screen.

  * The veil of the Temple was a curtain of blue, purple and scarlet material that was hung at the entrance to the most holy place in the Temple which contained the Ark of the Covenant.
  * A veil is used as a symbol of the flesh of Christ as in Hebrews 10:20.

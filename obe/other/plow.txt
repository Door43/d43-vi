====== plow ======

===== Definition: =====
A "plow" is a shop farm implement usually pulled by a pair of oxen used for cutting rows into fields for planting. 
 
  * Plows are pointed tools with handles used to break up the ground.
  * A "plow" can be any pointed tool used in agriculture to break, or scratch the ground. 
  * It can be pulled by animals or used by hand.

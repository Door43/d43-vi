====== gird ======

===== Definition: =====
To "gird" is to secure something, usually a belt around a waist. 

  * In the Bible to "gird up your loins" is to strap the bottom of your garment into your belt to allow ease of movement. 
  * "Girding" can also mean to surround something in a circle.

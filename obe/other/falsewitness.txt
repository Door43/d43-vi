====== false witness ======

===== Definition: =====

A false witness is someone who does not tell the truth when giving a report about something that happened.

  * To "bear false witness" means to give a false report about something.
  * The ninth commandment says to not bear false witness, that is, to not lie.
  * False witnesses were brought in to testify against Jesus. There were also false witnesses who told lies about Stephen, which led to his death.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====


  * To "bear false witness" could be translated as, "testify falsely" or "give a false report" or "speak a false testimony."
  * When "false witness" refers to a person, it could be translated as "person who lies" or "one who testifies falsely" or "someone who says things that are not true."

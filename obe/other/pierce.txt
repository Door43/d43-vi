====== pierce ======

===== Definition: =====
Pierce means to stab through something with a sharp object. 

  * On the cross Jesus' side was pierced with a spear.
  * In the Bible when a slave or a servant was set free but wanted to stay with their master their ear was pierced.

(See also: [[:En:obe:kt:cross]], [[:En:obe:kt:jesus]], [[:En:obe:other:servant]])

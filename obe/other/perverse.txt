====== perverse ======

===== Definition: =====

To behave or act in a way which is obviously unacceptable and inappropriate. 

  * In the Bible the Israelites acted perversely when they disobeyed God. 
  * Any action which is against the standards or behavior is considered perverse.

(See also: other)

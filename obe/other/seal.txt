====== seal, to seal ======

===== Definition: =====

A seal is a symbol of a particular entity; an individual or an organization can have its own seal as a form of representation.

  * To seal something means to close it and keep it closed with something that makes it impossible to open without breaking the seal.
  * Wax that was melted and then allowed to harden was often used to seal letters or other documents that needed to be protected.
  * A seal can also be a special mark that is used to identify someone as belonging to someone else

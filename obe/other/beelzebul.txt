====== Beelzebul ======


===== Facts: =====


Beelzebul is the ruler of the demons and another name for Satan.


    * Beelzebul is also spelled Beelzebub in some versions of the Bible.
    * Beelzebul can also be translated as "Satan" or, "the devil" or, "the evil one."

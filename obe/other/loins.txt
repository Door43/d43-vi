====== loins ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "loins" refers to the part of the body of an animal or person that is the lower part of the abdomen.

  * "Lions" in the Bible are often a symbol of strength and power. 
  * In some translations the term "loins" refers to producing offspring or to one's descendants.
  * The phrase "gird up your loins" is referring to tucking the bottom of a shirt into your belt in order to move with ease.

====== Joshua Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When the assembly of the people met at Shiloh, how many tribes of the people of Israel had not been assigned an inheritance?

A. When the assembly of the people met a Shiloh, seven tribes of the people of Israel had not been assigned an inheritance. [18:2]

Q? Who did Joshua send out to survey the land up and down?

A. Joshua sent out three men from each of the seven tribes to survey the land up and down. [18:4]

Q? What were the three men from each tribe to report to Joshua?

A. The three men from each tribe were to write out a description of the land they surveyed in seven sections and report back to Joshua. [18:4-6]

Q? What did Joshua do after the men returned to Joshua after making the survey?

A. Joshua cast lots for them at Shiloh before Yahweh when they returned from surveying the land. [18:10]

Q? Between what two tribes were the tribe of Benjamin assigned as an inheritance?

A. The tribe of Benjamin was assigned as an inheritance the land between the descendents of Judah and the descendents of Joseph.  [18:11]

====== Psalms Chapter 065 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To whom does David say their praise and vows will be carried?

A. Their praise and vows will be carried to God in Zion. [65:1]

Q? What does David say about their transgressions?

A. David says God will forgive them. [65:3]

Q? Who does David say is the blessed man?

A. Blessed is the man whom God chooses to bring near and live in his courts; he will be satisfied with the goodness of God's house. [65:4]

Q? How will God of their salvation answer their prayer?

A. God will answer in righteousness by doing amazing things. [65:5]

Q? How does David describe the actions of God?

A. David states God made the mountains firm, quieted the roaring seas, and the commotion of the peoples. [65:5,7]

Q? Who is afraid of God's deeds and who rejoices?

A. Those who live in the uppermost parts of the earth are afraid of God's deeds and God makes the east and west rejoice. [65:8]

Q? How does David say God helps the earth?

A. God waters it, enriches it, and prepares the earth to provide mankind grain. [65:9]

Q? How is abundant water used by the earth?

A. Water drops onto the pastures in the wilderness and the hills are dressed with joy. [65:10,12]

Q? With what are the pastures clothed, the valleys covered and how do they react?

A. The pastures are clothed with flocks, the valleys are covered over with grain, and they shout for joy and sing. [65:13]

====== Psalms Chapter 043 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the psalmist ask God to do?

A. He asks God to judge him and to plead his case against an ungodly nation. [43:1]

Q? Why should God judge him and plead his cause?

A. God should do this because God is the God of his strength. [43:2]

Q? What does the psalmist ask God to let his light and his truth do?

A.  He asks that God's light and truth lead him and bring him to God's holy hill and tabernacles. [43:3]

Q? Where will the psalmist go at that time?

A. Then he will go to the altar of God. [43:4]

Q? What will he do on his harp?

A. He will praise God on his harp. [43:4]

Q? Why should the psalmist's soul not be discouraged nor disquieted within himself?

A. He should hope in God for he will yet praise God, who is his help and his God. [43:5]

====== Psalms Chapter 063 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does David seek God?

A. He seeks God earnestly, his soul thirsts for him, and his flesh longs for God. [63:1]

Q? When David looks for God in the sanctuary, what does he see?

A. David sees God's power and glory. [63:2]

Q? What will David do because of God's covenant faithfulness?

A. David's lips will praise God, he will bless him while he lives, and he will lift up his hands in God's name. [63:3,4]

Q? What will David do because his soul is satisfied?

A. Davd's mouth will praise God with joyful lips when he is on his bed thinking and mediating in the night. [63:5,6]

Q? Why does David's soul cling to God?

A. David's soul clings to God becuse God has been his help and support. [63:7,8]

Q? What will happen to those who seek to destroy David's soul?

A. Those that seek to destroy his soul will go into the lower parts of the earth, they will be given over to the power of the sword, and given to jackals. [63:9,10]

Q? What will happen to those who swear by the king that rejoices in God?

A. Those that swear by him will be proud of him. [63:11]

Q? What will happen to those who speak lies?

A. Those that speak lies will have their mouths stopped. {63:11]

====== Psalms Chapter 061 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are the cries of David's heart?

A. David asks God to hear is cry, attend to his prayer when his heart is overwhelmed, and be led to the rock that is higher than him. [61:1,2]

Q? What has God been for David?

A. God has been a refuge and a strong tower from the enemy for David. [61:3]

Q? Where will David live and take refuge?

A. David will live in God's tabernacle forever and take refuge in the hiding place under God's wings. [61:4]

Q? What has God heard and given David?

A. God has heard David's vows and given him the inheritance of those who honor God's name. [61:5]

Q? Who will prolong the king's life and where will he remain?

A. God will prolong the king's life like many generations and he will remain before God forever. [61:6,7]

Q? Why will David sing praise to God's name forever?

A. David will sing praise to God's name forever so that he may perform his vows every day. [61:8]

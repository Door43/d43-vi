====== Psalms Chapter 052 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? David says that the mighty man is proud of making trouble, but what comes from God?

A. "The covenant faithfulness of God comes every day." [52:1]

Q? What kind of words does this mighty man love?

A. "You love all words that devour others." [52:4]

Q? David says that God will destroy that mighty man for how long?

A. "God will likewise destroy you forever." [52:5]

Q? In what did the man who did not make God his strength trust?

A. David said he trusted in the abundance of his riches. [52:7]

Q? By contrast David says that his own trust is placed in what?

A. "I will trust in the covenant faithfulness of God forever and ever." [52:8]

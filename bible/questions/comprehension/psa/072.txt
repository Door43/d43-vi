====== Psalms Chapter 072 ======

[[:vi:bible:questions:comprehension:psa:072?do=|Show page]]

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does King David pray to God for his son?

A. That David's son may judge God's people with righteousness and the poor with justice. [71:1-2]

Q? How long do they honor God?

A. They honor him through all generations.[72:5]

Q? What will happen during the king's son's days?

A. The righteous will flourish and there will be an abundance of peace. [72:6-7]

Q? What is the extent of his domain?

A. His domain is from sea to sea, from the River to the ends of the earth. [72:8]

Q? Who recognizes the king's domain?

A. Those who live in the wilderness, the kings of Tarshish, and the kings of Sheba and Seba will recognize the king. [72:9-10].

Q? What will all kings and nations do before the king's son?

A. All the kings will fall down before him and serve him. [72:11]

Q? How does the king see the people?

A. He has pity on the poor and needy, he redeems their soul from oppression and violence, and their blood is precious in his sight. [72:13-14]

Q? What are the signs of God's blessing on the king?

A. The signs of blessing are the gold from Sheba, his people praying for him ,an abundance of crops, and the people will flourish. [72:15-16]

Q? Why do the nations call the king blessed?

A. Because his name endures forever, and people are blessed in him. [72:17]

Q?  Whose glorious name does David bless forever?

A. David blesses Yahweh God, the God of Israel. [72:18-20]

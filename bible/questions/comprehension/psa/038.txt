====== Psalms Chapter 038 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why does David say, "there is no health in my bones?"

A. There is no health in David's bones because of his sin. [38:3]

Q? David is overcome with what?

A. I am overcome with shame. [38:7]

Q? What does David say the Lord understands?

A. Lord, you understand my heart's deepest yearnings. [38:9]

Q? Why do David's friends and companions shun him?

A. David's friends and companions shun him because of his condition. [38:11]

Q? What does David fear if his foot slips?

A. If David's foot slips, his enemies will do terrible things to him. [38:16]

Q? Although David pursued what is good, how do his enemies respond?

A. They repay David evil for good and they hurl accusations at him. [38:20]

Q? What is the Lord's name David uses when he asks him to come quickily to help?

A. "Come quickly to help me, Lord, my salvation." [38:22]

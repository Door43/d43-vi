====== Psalms Chapter 051 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? David asks for mercy from God based on what?

A. Have mercy on me, God, because of your covenant faithfulness. [51:1]

Q? David says he has sinned against whom?

A. Against you, you only, I have sinned. [51:4]

Q? When does David say that his sin began?

A. I was born in iniquity; as soon as my mother conceived me, I was in sin. [51:5]

Q? What does David ask God to do so that he may be purified?

A. He asks God to purify him with hyssop and wash him. [51:7]

Q? What does David ask God to create and renew in him?

A. Create in me a clean heart, God, and renew a right spirit within me. [51:10]

Q? What does David ask God to not do to him?

A. Do not drive me away from your presence, and do not take your holy Spirit from me. [51:11]

Q? When David says, "Forgive meâ€¦ I will shout for the joy," what sin is he asking for forgiveness for?

A. "Forgive me for shedding blood, God of my salvation." [51:14]

Q? David knows that God does not delight in sacrifice, what are the sacrifices of God?

A. "The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit; you will not despise a broken and a contrite heart, God." [51:17]

====== Psalms Chapter 020 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? From where does David want God to send help and support?

A. He asks Yahweh to send help from the sanctuary and support from Zion. [20:2]

Q? What does David ask Yahweh to call to mind and accept?

A. David asks Yahweh to call to mind all of the offerings and accept their burnt sacrifices. [20:3]

Q? What is it that David says causes rejoicing?

A. David says they will rejoice in Yahweh's salvation. [20:5]

Q? Who does David know that Yahweh will rescue?

A. David knows that Yahweh will rescue his anointed. [20:6]

Q? What does David say his people do that results in victory that others do not do?

A. David says, "We call on Yahweh our God." [20:7]

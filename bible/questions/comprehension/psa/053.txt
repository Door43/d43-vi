====== Psalms Chapter 053 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? David says that God looks down on the sons of mankind and finds how many of them who understand and seek after him?

A. "There is no one who does good, no, not one." [53:3]

Q? Why does David say that the sons of mankind who do not call on God will be put to shame?

A. "Such people will be put to shame because God has rejected them." [53:5]

Q? David expresses hope that salvation for Israel would come from where?

A. "Oh, that the salvation of Israel would come from Zion!" [53:6]

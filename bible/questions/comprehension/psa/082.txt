====== Psalms Chapter 082 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where does God stand to render judgment?

A. He stands in the divine assembly and in the midst of the gods. [82:1]

Q? What does the writer ask God?

A.He asks God how long God will you judge unjustly and show favoritism to the wicked? [82:2]

Q? What does the writer ask God to do for the poor and fatherless and the afflicted and destitute?

A. He asks God to defend them and maintain their rights. [82:3]

Q? What does the writer ask God to do for the poor and needy?

A. He asks God to rescue them and take them out of the hand of the wicked. [82:4]

Q? What will happen because of those who neither know nor understand and wander around in the darkness?

A. All the foundations of the earth will crumble. [82:5]

Q? How does the writer say the sons of the Most High will die?

A. They will die like men and fall like one of the princes. [82:6-7]

Q? Why is the writer asking God to arise and judge the earth?

A. God will inherit all the nations. [82:8]

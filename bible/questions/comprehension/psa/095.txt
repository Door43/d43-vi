====== Psalms Chapter 095 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the writer encourage everyone to do?

A. The writer encourages everyone to enter Yahweh's presence with thanksgiving and sing joyfully to him with psalms of praise. [95:1-2]

Q? How does Yahweh compare to other gods?

A. Yahweh is a great God and King who is superior to all gods. [95:3]

Q? How is Yahweh superior to all gods?

A. Yahweh is superior to all gods in that the heights of the mountains and the seas which he made are his and his hands formed the dry land. [95:4-5]

Q? Why should everyone worship by bowing down and kneeling before Yahweh?

A. Everyone should worship Yahweh because he is their God and they are people of his pasture and the sheep of his hand. [95:6-7]

Q? What response did the writer encourage from the people whose forefathers challenged Yahweh's authority and tried his patience?

A. The writer encouraged the people not to harden their hearts. [95:8-9]

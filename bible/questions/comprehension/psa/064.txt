====== Psalms Chapter 064 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What is David asking of God?

A. David wants God to hear his voice, listen to his complaint, preserve his life from his enemies, and hide him from the secret plotting of evildoers. [64:1,2]

Q? What does David say his enemies have done?

A. David's enemies have sharpened their tongues as swords, used their bitter words as arrows so that they may shoot at someone who is innocent and fear nothing. [64:3,4]

Q? What other things are his enemies doing?

A. They consult privately together to plan evil and set traps, saying "Who will see us?" [64:5]

Q? How will God respond to the enemy?

A. God will shoot them and suddenly they will be wounded with his arrows. [64:7]

Q? Because of God's actions, what will happen to the enemy?

A. The enemy will be made to stumble and all who see them will wag their heads. [64:8]

Q? How will all people respond?

A. All people will fear, declare God's deeds, and think wisely about what God has done. [64:9]

Q? What will the righteous and all the upright in heart do?

A. The righteous will be glad about Yahweh and will take refuge in him; all the upright in heart will take pride in him. [64:10]

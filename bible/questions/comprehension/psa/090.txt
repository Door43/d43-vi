====== Psalms Chapter 090 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How long has the Lord been Moses' refuge?

A. The Lord has been Moses' refuge throughout all generations before the Lord formed the earth and the world. [90:1-2]

Q? Why does the Lord say, "Return, you descendants of mankind?"

A. The Lord says that a thousand years in his sight are as yesterday when it is past, and as a watch in the night. [90:3-4]

Q? To what does Moses liken the descendants of mankind?

A. The descendants of mankind are like the grass that sprouts up, blooms and grows up, and then withers and dries up. [90:5-6]

Q? Why is mankind consumed in the Lord's anger and terrified in his wrath?

A. Mankind is consumed in the Lord's anger and terrified in his wrath because he has set their iniquities before himself and hidden their sins in the light of his presence. [90:7-8]

Q? How does Moses describe the life of mankind?

A. Mankind may live seventy or even eighty years if healthy, but even his best years are marked by trouble and sorrow. [90:9-10]

Q? What does Moses ask the Lord to do for mankind?

A. Moses asks the Lord to teach mankind to consider their lives, so that they may live wisely. [90:12]

Q? How does Moses want the Lord to satisfy mankind in the morning?

A. He wants the Lord to satisfy mankind with the Lord's covenant faithfulness, so that they may rejoice and be glad all their days. [90:14]

Q? What does Moses want the Lord to do for his servants?

A. He wants the Lord to let the servants see his work and their children see his majesty. [90:16]

Q? How does the Lord show his favor to mankind?

A. He prospers the work of his hands. [90:17]

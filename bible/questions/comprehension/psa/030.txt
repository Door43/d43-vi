====== Psalms Chapter 030 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why does David exalt Yahweh?

A. Yahweh has raised David up and not allowed David's enemies to rejoice over him. [30:1]

Q? What did Yahweh do when David cried for help?

A. Yahweh healed David [30:2]

Q? What did Yahweh do for David?

A. Yahweh brought up David's soul from sheol and kept him alive. [30:3]

Q? What are the faithful told to do in remembrance of Yahweh's holiness?

A. The faithful are told to sing praise to Yahweh and give him thanks. [30:4]

Q? How long does Yahweh's anger and favor last?

A.Yahweh's anger is for a moment and his favor is for a lifetime. [30:5]

Q? What happens after weeping comes for a night?

A. Joy comes in the morning. [30:5]

Q? What did David say in confidence?

A. David said, "I will never be shaken." [30:6]

Q? How did David feel about Yahweh?

A. Yahweh's favor established David as a strong mountainYahweh hid his face, David was troubled. [30:7]

Q? How did David seek favor from the Lord?

A. David cried to Yahweh. [30:8]

Q? What is David asking Yahweh about the advantage of David's death?

A. David asks, "Will the dust praise you? Will it declare your trustworthiness?" [30:9]

Q? What things is David asking of Yahweh?

A. David is asking Yahweh to hear him, have mercy on him and be his helper. [30:10]

Q? What has Yahweh done for David?

A. Yahweh has turned David's mourning into dancing, removed his sackcloth and clothed him with gladness. [30:11]

Q? What will David's honored heart do?

A. David's heart will sing praise to Yahweh and not be silent. He will give thanks to Yahweh his God forever. [30:12]

====== Psalms Chapter 060 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What has God done to them, and what is being requested of God?

A. He has cast them off, broken them down, and has been angry with them; God should restore them again. [60:1]

Q? What has God done to the land, and what is being requested of him?

A. He has made the land tremble and torn it apart; God should heal its breaches. [60:2]

Q? What has God shown them, and what has he made them drink?

A. He has shown them severe things, and made them drink the wine of staggering. [60:3]

Q? Who has been given a banner and why will it be displayed?

A. Those that honor God have been given a banner so that it may be displayed because of the truth. [60:4]

Q? Who may be rescued by God, and how will they be rescued?

A. Those that love God may be rescued with his right hand. [60:5]

Q? What has God spoken is his holiness?

A. God says he will rejoice, he will divide Shechem and apportion out the valley of Succoth. He says Gilead and Mannasseh are his, Ephraim is his helmet and Judah is his scepter. Moab is his washbasin. [61:6,7,8]

Q? What does God say he will do?

A. God will throw his shoe over Edom and he will shout in triumph because of Philistia.[60:8]

Q? Why does the writer say that God does not go into battle with his army?

A. The writer questions God's rejection of them because God did not go into battle with them. [60:10]

Q? Why is the writer asking for help from God?

A. The writer is asking for help because man's help is futlle, with God's help the enemy will be trampled down and the army will triumph. [60:11,12]

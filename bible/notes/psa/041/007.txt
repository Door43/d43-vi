===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **against me they hope for my hurt**  - AT: "they hope that very bad things will happen to me" (UDB)
    * **An evil disease**  - Two ideas are possible here: the disease is serious, and the disease is caused by sin or bad behavior.  (See:[[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **in whom I trusted**  - AT: "whom I trusted very much" (UDB) (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **has lifted up his heel against me**  - AT: "has betrayed me" (UDB)

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **you delight in me**  - AT: "you are pleased with me" (UDB)
    * **By this I know that you delight in me, for my enemy does not triumph over me**  - AT: "If you enable me to do that, with the result that my enemies do not defeat me, I will know that you are pleased with me" (See:[[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_order]])
    * **will keep me before your face**  - AT: "will enable me to be with you" (UDB) (See:[[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **you support me** - AT: "you have helped me" (UDB)

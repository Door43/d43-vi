===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I thought of God as I groaned; I thought about him as I grew faint**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **as I grew faint**  - AT: "as my spirit grew faint" or "as my spirit was overwhelmed"
  * **Selah** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:psa:003:001|3:2]].

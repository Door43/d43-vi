===== Translation Notes: =====

The person who sings this song continues to describe a person who reveres Yahweh.
  * **For he will never be moved** - AT: "Because he will never fail" or "Because nothing will ever overcome him." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the righteous person will be remembered forever** - AT: "people will remember a righteous person forever." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fervent**  - "devout" or "passionate"
  * **For he will stand at the right hand of the needy person **- AT: “Because Yahweh is close by to help the poor person.” The poor were (and are) especially vulnerable to oppressors, those who are able to deprive them of their rights. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

This psalm is a prayer for help.\\
See: [[vi:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **For the chief musician** - See how you translated this in [[:vi:bible:notes:psa:004:001|4:1]].
  * **on stringed instruments** - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This psalm is to be accompanied by someone playing a stringed instrument” or “A person should play a stringed instrument with this song”
  * **A psalm of David** - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of David or “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
  * **For the wicked and deceitful** - The words "wicked" and "deceitful" have similar meaning and are used together for emphasis. AT: "For wicked and deceitful men" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and  [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

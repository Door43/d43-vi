===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **deep and wide** - "it is deep and wide" This means that the sea is great in both directions, depth and width. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **teeming** - "overflowing"
  * **innumerable** - "uncountable"
  * **both small and great** - This refers to all creatures. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **The ships travel there** - "The ships travel on the sea"

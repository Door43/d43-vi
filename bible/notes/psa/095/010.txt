===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking directly to his people.

  * **forty years** - "40 years" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **wander astray** - "move away from me" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **known my ways** - AT: "obeyed me" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **ways ** - God's plans, precepts, purposes or commands
  * **resting place**  - "Canaan" or "Promised Land"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your hand** - This refers to Yahweh's power and control. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the man of your right hand** - This refers to the nation of Israel which Yahweh has chosen as his people. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **right hand** - The side of a person that is favored and gives honor to the one chosen to stand next to that person. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

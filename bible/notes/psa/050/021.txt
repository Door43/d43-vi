===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I was someone just like yourself** - AT: "I was someone just like yourself who accepts the sins of adulterers and thieves" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **reprove** - See how you translated this word in [[vi:bible:notes:psa:050:007|50:8]].
  * **right before your eyes** - This phrase refers to something that is easy to understand because it is in a place that is easy to see. AT: "in a way that is easy to understand" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you who forget God** - It is like the people have forgotten God because they do not consider as obey his commands. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will tear you in pieces** - This describes someone being killed in a very violent way.  It is used to emphasize God's power if he chooses to destroy them. AT: "I will destroy you" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **A psalm of Asaph** - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of Asaph” or “This is a psalm that Asaph wrote”
    * **The Mighty One, God, Yahweh** - The author refers to God in three different ways to add emphasis to his importance.
    * **from the rising of the sun to its setting** - This refers to the whole earth and everyone in it. AT: "from where to the sun rises to where it sets" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
    * **the perfection of beauty** - AT: "the most beautiful city"
    * **God has shone** - AT: "The glory of God shines like the sun" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])

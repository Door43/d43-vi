===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[vi:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **For the chief musician** - "For the leader of the music team"
  * **the rhythm of the deer** - A possible meaning is that this refers to the musical tune or beat.
  * **A psalm of David** - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of David” or “This is a psalm that David wrote”
  * **my God, why have you abandoned me?** - The writer does not really expect an answer. AT: "my God, I feel very abandoned by you!" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **you abandoned me** - "you left me all alone"
  * **Why are you so far from saving me and far from the words of my anguish?** - Again the writer does not expect an answer. AT: "you are far from saving me and far from the words of my anguish!" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the words of my anguish** - "the words of my suffering" AT: "me while I am suffering" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I am not silent** - AT: "I still speak" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])

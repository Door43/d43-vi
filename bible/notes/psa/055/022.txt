===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your, you -**  David is speaking to other godly people.
  * **he **- Refers to God.
  * **a righteous person to fall.**- AT: "the righteous to be moved from his faith."
  * **vs 23 **- David turns to talk directly to God.
  * **bloodthirsty**  - This is someone who is eager to hurt and enjoys the sight of violence and bloodshed.
  * **I - ** David speaks for himself.

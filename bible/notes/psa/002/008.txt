===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will give you the nations for an inheritance ... and the farthermost regions of the earth for your possession** - Both of these clauses express the same idea. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the nations** - AT: "rule over the nations" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the farthermost regions of the earth** - AT: "the lands that are very far away" 
  * **You will break them with an iron scepter; you will smash like a potter's jar** - AT: "You will defeat them completely; you will totally destroy them." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and   [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]) - AT: "You will defeat them completely" (See:[[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

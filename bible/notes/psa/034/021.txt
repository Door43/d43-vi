  * **Evil will kill the wicked** - Evil is described as if it were a man who can kill people. AT: "Evil people will be killed because of their own wicked deeds" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **none of those who take refuge in him will be condemned** - AT: "everyone who takes refuge in him will be forgiven" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])

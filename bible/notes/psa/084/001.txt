===== Translation Notes: =====

This is a psalm, which is a song, by the sons of Korah.  See: [[vi:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **Gittith**  - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:psa:008:001|8:1]].
  * **lovely **  - "beautiful"
  * **My soul yearns, even pines, for the courts of Yahweh;**  - "I really want to be in the courts of Yahweh;"
  * **whole being** - "flesh" or "whole body"

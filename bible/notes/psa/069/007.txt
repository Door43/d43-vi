===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **rebuke** - to express disapproval in a very strong manner
    * **For your sake I have borne rebuke** - This can be translated as "I was rebuked by people for your sake" (See: Explicit and Implicit)
    * **shame has covered my face** - This means that he felt very ashamed.  It was as if his face was covered in shame and everyone could see it.  (See: Personification)
    * **I have become a stranger to my brothers, an alien to my mother’s children** - These two phrases are saying same thing. They mean that David felt like a stranger among his family. (See: Doublet)
    * **the zeal of your house has eaten me up**  - The term “zeal” can be translated as “energetic devotion”  or “righteous enthusiasm.” David is saying that his whole life is devoted to the worship of God (See: Hyperbole, Personification)

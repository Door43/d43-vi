===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **by their own sword** - The word "sword" refers to the military power. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your arm** - Yahweh's arm refers to his power. AT: "your power" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the light of your face** - This part of Yahweh refers to his guidance. AT: "your guidance" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **victory for Jacob** - The people of Israel are referred to by the name of their ancestor "Jacob". (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

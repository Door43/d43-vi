===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer assumes that the reader knows the history of how David became the chosen king. (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])

  * **My hand will support him; my arm will strengthen him.** - "Hand" and "arm" both means the power and control of Yahweh. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **son of wickedness** - The writer refers to those who have the nature or character of wickedness as "sons of wickedness." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

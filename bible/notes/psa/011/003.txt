===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For if the foundations are ruined, what can the righteous do?** - AT: "What can the righteous do when evil people are not punished when they disobey the laws?" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **children of men** - "humanity"
  * **his eyes watch, his eyes examine the children of men** - AT: "He examines all that humanity does." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])

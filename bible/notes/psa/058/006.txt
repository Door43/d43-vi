===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **vs 6 **- Word order changed from ULB to UDB, but the meaning is the same.
  * **their, their, vs 8 them**  - Refers to wicked people.
  * **young lions**  - (See Anthromorphism) This is a way to refer to David's enemies.
  * **melt away as water**  - (See [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **vs 7 c, them**  - Refers to the arrows without points.

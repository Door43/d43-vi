===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Whom have I in heaven but you? **- This sentence shows that God is all Asaph wants or needs. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **My flesh and my heart  **- These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. Together they represent all of the person. AT: "My body and my mind" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])

===== Translation Notes: =====
The author continues to speak to the queen.

  * **The daughter of Tyre** - This is a metaphor for the people of Tyre. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **royal daughter** - AT: "the young woman the king will marry"
  * **her clothing is worked with gold**  - AT: "tailors use gold to make her clothes"

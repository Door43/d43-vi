===== Translation Notes: =====
See: [[vi:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **maschil** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:psa:032:001|32:1]].
  * **Shoshannim** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:psa:045:001|45:1]].
  * **A song of loves** - "A love song"
  * **overflows** - "is overwhelmed" or "is excited"
  * **a good subject** - "a noble theme" or "a good word"
  * **composed**  - to have written or created a song
  * **my tongue is the pen of a ready writer**  - The author's tongue is compared to a pen. The author refers to himself by his tongue, because that is the part of his boy he speaks with. AT: "My tongue is like the pen of person who writes well" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **You are fairer than the children of men** - This phrase exaggerates how the king looks, saying that he is better looking than all the children born of men. AT: "you are better looking than most other children born of men" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **grace is poured onto your lips**  - The lips of the king refers to his ability to speak. Poured grace expresses that God has greatly blessed the king's ability to speak. AT: "you speak gracefully" or "you speak beautifully" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

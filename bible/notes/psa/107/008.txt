===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Oh that people would praise Yahweh** - This is a third person command "Let people praise Yahweh" or "People should praise Yahweh"
  * **for his covenant faithfulness** - "because he faithfully loves them"
  * **satisfies those** - “satisfies people”
  * **and those who are hungry he fills with good things** - “and he gives hungry people good things to eat.”
  * **for humanity** - "for all people" 
  * **Some sat** - It is implied that these are people that Yahweh has rescued. AT: "Yahweh also rescued people who sat." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in gloom** - "in complete darkness"

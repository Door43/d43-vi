===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See, I was born in iniquity; as soon as my mother conceived me, I was in sin.**- AT: " I was born in sin; I was a sinner at the moment my mother conceived me" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **my mother conceived me**  - "my mother became pregnant with me"
  * **See, you desire trustworthiness in my heart; in my heart you will make me know wisdom**- AT: " You desire honesty in my heart and you teach me wisdom"

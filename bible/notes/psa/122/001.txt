===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[vi:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **Song of Ascents**  - See how you translated this in [[:vi:bible:notes:psa:120:001|120:1]]
  * **of David** - "written by David" (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **our feet are** - Here the people are being referred to by their feet since people stand on their feet. AT: "We are" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **inside your gates, Jerusalem** - AT: in the city of Jerusalem" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
  * **Jerusalem was build like a city that is compact together** - AT: "Jerusalem was built with great unity"

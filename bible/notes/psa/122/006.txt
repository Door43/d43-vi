===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They will prosper who love you.** - "Let those who love you prosper"
  * **May there be peace within ... and prosperity within ...** - These two phrases have similar meanings and are used together to strengthen each other. AT: "May there be peace and prosperity within Jerusalem" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **peace within your walls** - Here Jerusalem is referred to by its walls. AT: "peace within Jerusalem" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **prosperity within your towers**- This refers to the Jerusalem and likely the surrounding areas within Jerusalem's control. At: "may there be prosperity in Jerusalem" or "may there be prosperity in Jerusalem and its surroundings"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **make a highwayâ€¦valley **- This phrase can be translated "The people should make it easy for God to be in their lives".  (See: Metaphor)
  * **a father of the fatherless**- This can also be translated "God acts like a father to children who have no parents". (See: Metaphor)
  * **a judge of the widows**  - This can also be translated "God protects widows." (See: Metaphor)
  * **the lonely into families - **  This can also be translated "Even when we have no earthly family, God treats us as family members." (See: Metaphor)
  * **he brings out the prisoners with singing - **  This can also be translated "God frees prisoners to be successful." (See: Metaphor)
  * **rebellious live in a parched land **- This can also be translated "Those who rebel against God are alone, without family, and unprotected. They are prisoners of their sins, and have no resources to get out of their condition." (See: Metaphor)

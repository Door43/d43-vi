===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Relent** - AT: "Have mercy,"
  * **in death there is no remembrance of you ... In sheol who will give you thanks?** - Both of these clauses express similar meanings. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **In sheol, who will give you thanks?** - The speaker puts this remark in the form of a question to add emphasis. AT: "The dead cannot praise you!" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:vi:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **For the chief musician**  - AT: "Send this to the priest in charge of the worship music."
  * **for Jeduthun** - See how to translated names: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]].
  * **I will watch what I say- **  AT: "I will pay attention to the things that I say." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **so that I do not sin with my tongue**  - AT: "so that I do not speak an offense against Yahweh." Tongue represents the whole person. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **muzzle** - To "muzzle" means to keep a mouth shut. Here David means to keep from speaking while with an evil person. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

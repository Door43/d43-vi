===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See** - This word functions to show that a new theme begins in the Psalm. AT: "Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you”
  * **Yahweh's eye** - AT: "Yahweh's attention" or "Yahweh" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **who count on** - "who wait for" or "who hope for"
  * **to save their soul** - AT: "to save them" or "to protect them" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])

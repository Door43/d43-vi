===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my bones were wasting away** - AT: "I was wasting away" or "I was getting weaker" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **all day long** - AT: "continually" or "all the time" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **day and night** - AT: "all the time" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **your hand was heavy upon me** - AT: "you made me suffer heavily" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **My strength withered as in summer drought** - David's strength is compared to a small, green plants that turns brown and crumbles in the dry season. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Selah** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:psa:003:001|3:2]].

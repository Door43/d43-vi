===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A psalm of David**- This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of David" or “This is a psalm that David wrote”
  * **my soul thirsts for you, and my flesh longs...no water**:- David really needs God. He compares his need for God to his need for water. (See: Idiom)

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **wrongfully rejoice over me**  - AT: "rejoice to bring me pain when I have done them no wrong"
    * **carry out their wicked schemes**  - AT: "work their evil plans to my hurt"
    * **they do not speak peace**  - AT: "their words do not promote friendship" 
    * **devise deceitful words against those who are unsuspecting** - AT: "plot twisted words to trap those who have no reason to expect evil"

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Aha, we have what we wanted**  - AT: "Yes, we have gotten rid of him just like we desired" (See:Euphemism)
    * **May those who taunt me be covered with shame and dishonor**  - AT: "Wrap those who taunt me in shame and dishonor" (active/passive) 
    * **taunt** - This means to say insulting things to someone in order to make that person angry.

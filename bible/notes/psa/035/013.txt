===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **when they were sick**  - "They" refers to the unrighteous witnesses.
    * **I went about in grief...; I bent down in mourning....**  - (See parallelism)
    * **in grief as for my brother**  - AT: ...grieving as if it were my own brother who was ill. (See simile)
    * **I bent down in mourning as for my mother.**  - AT: I mourned for them as if my own mother had died. (See simile)

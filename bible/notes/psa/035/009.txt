===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **joyful in Yahweh**  - Meaning: faith in Yahweh makes me joyful.
    * **With all my strength**  - AT: "With my whole heart..."
    * **"Yahweh, who is like You,...**  - All of verse 10 is a rhetorical question.

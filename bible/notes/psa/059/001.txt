===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Al Tashheth** - This was likely the name of a musical melody. (See: [[[[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **michtam **- See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:psa:016:001|16:1]].
  * **when Saul sent, and they watched the house to kill him** - "from when Saul sent his soldiers to go and watch David's house for an opportunity to kill him"
  * **set me on high **- AT: "put me in a safe place" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **bloodthirsty men** - The word "bloodthirsty" contains "blood," which refers to murder and "thirsty," which refers to desire. AT: "the men who desire to kill" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do they not know anything, those who commit inquity**  - This question is not asked for getting information. No answer is expected (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **those who eat up my people as they eat bread**  - AT: "they devour my people as though eating bread" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **God will scatter the bones**  - AT: "God will destroy them" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Let them...who pursue my life to destroy it**  - "Let those who pursue my life to destroy it be put to shame and confused together." AT: "Shame and confuse those who are trying to kill me."
  * **Let them ... who delight in hurting me** - "Let those who delight in hurting me be turned back and brought to dishonor." AT: "Turn back those who delight in hurting me and dishonor them"
  * **Let them ... who say to me, "Ha ha ha"** - "Let  those who say to me, 'Ha ha ha' be shocked because of their shame"
  * **Let them be shocked because of their shame**  - TA: "I hope that they will be shocked when you shame them"
  * **those who say to me, "Ha ha ha"** - "Ha ha ha" is something people say when they are ridiculing someone. If your language has something that people say when ridiculing someone, it can be used here. This can also be translated with a verb like "ridicule." AT: "those who ridicule me" or "those who make fun of me" (UDB)

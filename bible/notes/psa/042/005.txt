===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why are you discouraged, my soul?**  - The author refers to himself as "my soul". He asks a question meaning that he should not be discouraged. AT: "I should not be discouraged." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **for the help of his presence**  - "for being with me and helping me"
  * **therefore I call you to mind** - "I think of you" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * ** from the land of the Jordan** -  "there in my homeland of Israel where the Jordan River flows down" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **peaks**  - mountain tops
  * **hill of Mizar**  - This is the name of a hill at the base of Mount Hermon. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])

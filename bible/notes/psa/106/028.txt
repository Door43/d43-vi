===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Peor**  - This is the name of a mountain. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **to the dead** - "The dead" refers to the idols and gods that the Israelites were worshiping. AT: "to gods who are dead" or "to lifeless gods" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a plague broke out**  - "a plague spread" 
  * **provoked him to anger** - AT: "angered him"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Into your hands I entrust my spirit** - AT: "so I put myself into your care" (UDB) or "I depend on you to take care of me." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **God of trustworthiness** - "you are a God I can trust"
  * **I hate those who serve worthless idols** - "Idols are worthless. I hate those who serve them." 
  * **you have seen my affliction ... you have known the distress of my soul** - Both of these phrases express the idea that God knows about the writer's troubles. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

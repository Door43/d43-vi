===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Lord is at your right hand** - When the lord goes to battle, Yahweh stands at his right hand in order to help him. AT: "The Lord helps you in battle" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The Lord** - The word "Lord" refers to Yahweh.
  * **your right hand** - David is speaking to the lord, whom Yahweh has made king.
  * **He will kill** - The word "He" refers to the Yahweh who does these things through the king.   
  * * **the kings** - "the enemy kings" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **on the day of his anger** - AT: "on the day of judgment when his patience turns to anger" (See: Implicit/Explicit)

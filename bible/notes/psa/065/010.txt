===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **You** - The word "you" refers to Yahweh
    * **its furrows**  - The word "its" refers to the earth that Yahweh has helped the laborers prepare for sowing the grain
    * **furrow**  - a long narrow gutter made in the ground for planting seeds or for watering the field where grains have been planted
    * **ridges** - edges
    * **crown the year with goodness**  - This means that Yahweh blesses the work of the laborers and rewards them with good harvest. (See: Personification)
    * **drop fatness down to the earth**  - The word "fatness" suggests fertile or goodness.  As used in this phrase, it points to how Yahweh makes the soil so good and fertile that it produces abundant harvest. (See: Metaphor) 
    * **hills are dressed with a belt of joy**  - This phrase is a picture of hills that look so beautiful, it is as though they are dressed and surrounded with joy. (See: Personification)

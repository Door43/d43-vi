===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **uttermost**  - very far away
    * **evidence** - proof or something that shows that another thing is true.
    * **the river of God is full of water**  - This phrase refers to the abundance of God's resources, including  rivers, which he fills with water.
    * **you provide mankind grain when you have prepared the earth**  - "when you have prepared the earth, it produces grain for all the people of the earth"

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **you who are the confidence of all the ends of the earth**  - "you are the confidence of all the ends of the earth"
    * **you are the confidence**  - The abstract noun "confidence" can be translated as “complete assurance”. AT: "you are the trusted one" (See: Abstract Nouns)
    * **of all the ends of the earth ... across the sea**  - "by people who live throughout the earth and across the sea"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **nor my life with bloodthirsty men**  - AT: "and do not sweep away my life with bloodthirsty men" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **bloodthirsty men**  - AT: "men who are eager to shed others' blood" or "people who are eager to kill others"
  * **in whose hands there is a plot**  - "people who plan to do wicked things"
  * **a plot**  - "a wicked plan"

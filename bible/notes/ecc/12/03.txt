===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **That will be the time**  - This is referring to old age.
  * **palace guard tremble**  - AT: "palace guard become weak"
  * **the women who grind cease because they are few,**  - Women were traditionally the ones who would grind wheat. But as they grew old and died, there were fewer to do the work, until those who remained stopped working.
  * **and those who look**  - AT: "and the eyes of those who look"

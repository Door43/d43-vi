===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A good name** - AT: "A good reputation" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the living must must take this to heart** - They need to consider what kind of name or reputation that they have and will leave behind when they die. AT: "those who are alive must think seriously about this".

===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to Ezekiel.
  * **David my servant** - See how you translated "my servant David" in [[vi:bible:notes:ezk:34:22|34:23]].
  * **over them** - AT: "over the people of Israel"
  * **one shepherd over them** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:ezk:34:22|34:23]]. 
  * **will walk according to my decrees** - AT: "will live as I have commanded"
  * **they will keep my statutes and obey them** - See how you translated "keeps all my statutes, and he does them" in [[vi:bible:notes:ezk:18:19|18:19]]. 
  * **had stayed** - AT: "had lived"
  * **chief** - See how you translated "prince" in [[vi:bible:notes:ezk:07:26]])

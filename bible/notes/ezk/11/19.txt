===== Translation Notes: =====

God continues the prophecy of what will happen to the scattered Israelites.

  * **the stone heart from their flesh** - This is a way of saying that God will change their attitude from stubborn rebellion. AT: "their stubbornness" (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a heart of flesh** - This is a way to say that God will change their attitude to accept his rule. AT: "a willingness to obey" (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **affection** - AT: "devotion"
  * **detestable things** - AT: "obnoxious things" or "offensive things"
  * **conduct** - AT: "actions"
  * **on their own heads** - AT: "back onto those people." (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **declaration** - AT: "statement"

===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to teach the people of Israel why his way is fair.

  * **How is my way not fair, house of Israel? **- AT: "My way is certainly fair, people of Israel." (See [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **How is your way not fair? - **  AT: "It is your ways that are not fair" (See [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Each man among you according to his own way **- AT: "the actions of each man."
  * **so that they will not** -  "they" refers to all the transgressions of the people of Israel.

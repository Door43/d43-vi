===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh speaking about his desire for wicked people.

  * **Do I greatly rejoice in the death of the wicked, . . . and not in his turning away from his way so he will live?**  - AT: "I do not greatly rejoice in the death of the wicked, but I do rejoice if he turns away from his evil actions and lives" (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:vi:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **not in his turning away from his way​** - AT: "not changing from his sinful actions to do what is right. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

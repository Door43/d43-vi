===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Riblah**  - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **they passed sentence on him**  - AT: "decided what they would do to punish him" (UDB). Here "sentence" means a decision made by a court or by the king of how to punish someone. 
    * **they slaughtered them before his eyes**  - "they killed them while King Zedekiah was forced to watch"
    * **Then he put out his eyes**  - AT: "Then the Babylonian soldiers cut out Zedekiah's eyes"

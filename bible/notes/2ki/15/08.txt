===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Azariah, king of Judah** - Azariah was also known as "Uzziah."
  * **Zechariah the son of Jeroboam reigned over Israel in Samaria for six months** - AT: "Zechariah the son of Jeroboam was king of Israel for 6 months"
  * **evil in the sight of Yahweh** - “evil according to Yahweh” or “evil before Yahweh” (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **by which he had caused Israel to sin** - "by which​ Jeroboam caused the people of Israel to sin."

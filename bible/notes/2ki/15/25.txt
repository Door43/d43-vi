===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pekah, Remaliah, Argob, and Arieh** - (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) Argob and Arieh are not listed by name in the UDB; they are two of the "fifty men." 
  * **his captain** - "Pekahiah's captain," or AT: "a captain in King Pekahiah's army," 
  * **conspired** - To conspire is to make a secret plan to do harm to someone or something, in this case, King Pekahiah.
  * **fifty men** - "50 men" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **citadel of the king's palace** - "fortified place in the king's palace" (UDB)

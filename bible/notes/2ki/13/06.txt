===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they did not leave behind the sins of the house of Jeroboam**  - AT: "Israel continued to commit the same sins as Jeroboam had,"
  * **made them like the chaff at threshing time** - Chaff is the outer part of a grain seed that has no value and is thrown away on the ground. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **them** - "the army of Jehoahaz"

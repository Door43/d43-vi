===== Translation Notes: =====

King Hezekiah continues praying to Yahweh after receiving the letter from King Sennacherib of Assyria.

    * **I implore you**  - AT: "I beg of you"; To implore means to plead or beg.
    * **from his power**  - "from the power of the King of Assyria" (UDB) or "from the armies of the King of Assyria" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

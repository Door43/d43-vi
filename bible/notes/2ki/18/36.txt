===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **did not respond**  - AT: "did not say anything back to Rabshakeh"
    * **Eliakim, Hilkiah, Shebna, Joah, Asaph**  - (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **who was over the household**  - AT: "who managed the king's palace"
    * **the recorder**  - "the royal historian" or "the history keeper"
    * **with their clothes torn**  - In their culture, it was a sign of distress, grief or pain to tear their outer garments.

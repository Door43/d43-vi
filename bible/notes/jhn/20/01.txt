===== Translation Notes: =====

This is the third day after Jesus was buried.

  * **first day of the week**  - AT: "Sunday"
  * **disciple whom Jesus loved**  - This phrase appears to be the way that John refers to himself throughout his book.
  * **They have taken**  - "Someone has taken"

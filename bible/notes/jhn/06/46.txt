===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus now continues speaking to all those listening to him ([[:vi:bible:notes:jhn:06:32|6:32]]).

  * **Not that any man has seen the Father,...he has seen the Father** - Possible meanings: 1) John is adding his own words. You can use words that show that this is an important part of the story here. (See: [[vi:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:discourse:background]]) or 2) Jesus is correcting possible misunderstandings of [[:vi:bible:notes:jhn:06:43|6:45]].
  * **Truly, truly** - Translate this as you did in [[vi:bible:notes:jhn:01:49|1:51]].

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Truly, truly** - You can translate it the same way you did in [[vi:bible:notes :jhn:03:03|3:3]].
    * **born of water and the Spirit** - There are 3 possible meanings: 1) "baptised in water" or 2) "born physically" or 3) "born of the Holy Spirit" - AT: "born spiritually by the Holy Spirit" (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])

===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues telling the people what God commanded them to do.
  * **Every skilled man**- "Every man with a skill"
  * **clasp** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:exo:26:04|26:6]].
  * **bases** - These are heavy objects that rest on the ground and keep the object attached to them from moving. See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:exo:25:31|25:31]].
  * **atonement lid** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:exo:25:15|25:17]].

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **at midnight**  - AT: "in the middle of the night"
  * **cattle**  - Besides cattle, this can also mean sheep, goats, horses, and/or donkeys. (See: [[:vi:obe:other:livestock|livestock]])
  * **There was loud lamenting in Egypt**  - "All the Egyptians cried loudly" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **for there was not a house where there was not someone dead**  - AT: "because in every house someone's child had died." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes|Litotes]])

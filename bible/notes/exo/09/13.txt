===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **on you yourself**  - This means that even Pharaoh will be hurt by the plagues. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **I will do this so that you may know**  - The word "this" refers to the plagues that Moses had just told Pharaoh about.

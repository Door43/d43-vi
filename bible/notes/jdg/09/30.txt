===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Zebul**  - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:jdg:09:28|Judges 9:28]].
  * **Gaal**  - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:jdg:09:26|Judges 9:26]].
  * **Ebed**  - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:jdg:09:26|Judges 9:26]].
  * **his anger was kindled**  - "his anger was great" or "his anger ignited"
  * **in order to deceive**  - This means messengers were sent to Abimelech for the purpose of deceiving him.
  * **stirring up the city**  - This means that Gaal and his relatives are going to make a disturbance. The people of the city are agitated. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])

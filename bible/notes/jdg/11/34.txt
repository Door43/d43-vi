===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tambourines**  - This is a musical instrument that has a circular frame with with pairs of small metal jingles along the frame.
  * **crushed me with sorrow**  - This means that he felt so badly that he felt his heart was broken into pieces. AT: "broke my heart"

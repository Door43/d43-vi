===== Translation Notes: =====

In these verses, the Levites continue to praise Yahweh in the presence of the people of Israel. 

   * **while they enjoyed your great goodness** - "while they enjoyed the richness of life you provided"   
   * **they did not serve you** - "they were not obedient to your law or teaching"

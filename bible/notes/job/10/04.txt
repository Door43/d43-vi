===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do you have eyes of flesh? Do you see like a man sees?**- Job says the same thing twice for emphasis. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Do you have eyes of flesh?** - AT: "You do not have eyes like a man." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Do you see like a man sees?** - AT: "Your understanding is greater than our own."  (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Are your days like the days of mankind ... your years like the years of people** - Both of these lines have the same meaning.  (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **that you inquire after my iniquity ... search after my sin** - Both of these lines have the same meaning. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **from your hand** - AT: "from your power." (UDB) (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

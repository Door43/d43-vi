===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **if I am wicked** - "if I am guilty" or "if I do evil"
  * **even if I were righteous** - "if I am innocent"
  * **I could not lift up my head** - "I still must bow my head and feel ashamed" (UDB) (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **since I am filled with disgrace** - The word "disgrace" refers to Job's shame. AT: "Since I am ashamed"
  * **and am looking at my own suffering** - AT: "and feel miserable." (UDB)
  * **if my head lifts itself, you hunt me down like a lion** - AT: "if I become proud, you hunt me like a lion hunts its prey;" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **show yourself powerful** - "show your marvelous power"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now a certain matter was secretly brought to me; my ear received a whisper about it.** - These are separate sentences expressing the same idea in a different way.  They convey the idea that Eliphaz heard a message whispered to him. This repetition is a form of Hebrew poetry used frequently for emphasis, teaching, or clarity. (see: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **was secretly brought** - "something brought a certain matter to me secretly" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **in thoughts** - "amidst troubling thoughts"
  * **visions in the night** - "dreams"

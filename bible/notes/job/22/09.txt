===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the arms of the fatherless have been broken - This expression means, "the orphans were oppressed". (See: Hyperbole)**
  * There is darknes so you cannot see - This expression means there is moral blindness in Job because of a cold, callous heart.
  * **waters cover you - Waters here refer to judgement.(see: Metanymy)**

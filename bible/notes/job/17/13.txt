===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **couch - This term refers to the special place where he sleeps.**
  * **in the darkness - This expression refers to Sheol.**
  * **corruption, the worm - These are ways of speaking of death. Corruption refers to a decaying body, and worms speak of worms that eat dead things.**
  * **As for my hope, who can see any - He is saying, I have no hope of living a productive, satisfying life here any longer. (See: Rhetorical question)**

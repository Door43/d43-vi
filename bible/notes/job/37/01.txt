===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Verse 1**  : my heart trembles - This expression means, "I am afraid". (See: Idiom)
  * **it is moved out of its place - This expression means that his heart is pounding or beating very fast, in reaction to his fear.**
  * **sound of his voice, the sound that goes out of his mouth - The thunder is described as coming from God's mouth. (See: Metaphor and Personification)**
  * **to the borders of the earth- This describes the world as having an edge or border, so being limited.(See: Metaphor)**

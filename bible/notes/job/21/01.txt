  * diligently - This word here means for them to listen carefully to what Job says.
  * **consolation - This word means comfort or solace they would offer to Job.**
  * **suffer me - This expression in the context means "please allow me" to speak. It means for them to be patient  and allow him to speak as they carefully listen.**

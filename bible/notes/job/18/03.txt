===== Translation Notes: =====

  * stupid - In this context stupid means morally corrupt or foolish.

  * as beasts - This is comparing them to animals . (See: Simile)
  * verse 3 - This is a rhetorical question saying, "we are not stupid as you think!".  (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **Verse 4 - Again this is a rhetorical question to reject Job"s argument and put him down, making fun of him.**

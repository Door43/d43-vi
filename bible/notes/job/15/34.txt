===== Translation Notes: =====

  * They conceive mischief—- in ULB - This is a metonymy. The person's life is compared to the process of reproduction, whereby evil and deceit are being produced,(See:Metonymy) The UDB switches this figure of speech to "heart", but the meaning is the same.

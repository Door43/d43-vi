===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **pass away like the foam - This is a comparison in time. Foam quickly disapears from on the water and these people quickly disappear from the earth.(See: Simile)**
  * **Drought and Heat – , So Sheol –. - This is a comparison between drought and heat that consume the waters of melting snow and ice, so Sheol will consume sinful men.**

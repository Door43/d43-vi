===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like an army through a wide hole in the city wall - This compares Job to a city under attack with a hole in it defensive wall, and the enemy is charging through that hole. (See:Simile)**
  * **my honor is driven away as by the wind - This expression means that Job feels like his honor is disappearing like the wind blows something away.(See:Simile)**
  * **my prosperity passes as a cloud This expression means that Jobs material possessions are disappearing in a manner similar to a cloud slowly drifting away in the sky.(See:Simile)**

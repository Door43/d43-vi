===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Verse 11 **- The first and second part of the verse form a doublet, to emphasize what he is saying. (See: Doublet)
  * smashed me to pieces - This expression says that his life is "ruined", like a glass that is dropped on the floor and breaks. (See: metaphor)
  * **target - This means something or someone to attack and criticize. (See: metaphor)**

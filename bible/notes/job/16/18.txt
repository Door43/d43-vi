  * **Earth do not cover up my blood - This expression means, "I want it to be known that I unfairly died". (See: Euphemism)**
  * **let my cry have no resting place - This expression means, "Let what I say be very clear to everyone". (See: Litotes)**
  * **vouches - This means "to verify by supplying proof".**

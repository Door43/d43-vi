===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Verse 31- foam up like a pot **- This expression means that as he passes through the water he leaves a trail of bubbles behind him, like the bubbling of a pot of boiling water.(See: Simile)
  * **Verse 31-like a pot of ointment **- This expression means that he churns the sea like a jar of ointment would look after it was shaken thoroughly.(See: Simile)
  * **Verse 32- the deep to be white**- This expression means that the water is all riled up as Leviathan swims by because of his size,causing a trail of bubbles.
  * Verse 32 is a doublet, with each clause saying essentually the same thing.(See: Doublet)

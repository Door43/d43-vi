===== Translation Notes: =====

The young woman continues to describe her dream.
  * **I opened the door for my beloved** - "I opened for my beloved." Possible meanings are 1) AT: "I opened the door for my beloved" or 2) "I opened up my vulva for my beloved." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **my beloved** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:sng:01:12|1:13]].
  * **My heart sank, I became despondant.** - "My soul left, when he spoke." AT: "When he spoke, I felt like I had died." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

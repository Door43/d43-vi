===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **His arms are rounded gold set with jewels** -  "His hands are like rods of gold set with rare stones." She implies they are pure and strong. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his abdomen is ivory** - "his midsection is as white as ivory" 
  * **ivory** - "Ivory" is a hard white substance that comes from the tusk of elephants or other large animal.
  * **sapphires** - a costly blue or purple gem stone

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **His legs are pillars of marble** - Pillars of marble were the strongest weight-bearing building material in ancient time. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his appearance is like Lebanon, choice as the cedars** - Cedar trees from Lebanon are tall, straight trees used in building. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **choice as the cedars** - AT: "the finest and best cedar trees"

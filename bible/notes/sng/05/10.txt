===== Translation Notes: =====
 
  * **radiant** - "brilliant," "bright," or "sparkling"
  * **ruddy** - having a healthy red color on the cheeks
  * **outstanding among ten thousand** - "the best of ten thousand" or "the most important among 10,000." People notice him. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]) 
  * **the purest gold** - She is not saying he is actually made out of gold, but that he is the finest and most valuable substance known to man, without flaws or imperfection. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **as black as a raven** - His hair is compared to the black feathers of a raven, a large bird. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])

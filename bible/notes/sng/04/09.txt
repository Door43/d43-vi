===== Translation Notes: =====

   * **have stolen my heart** - He is saying his affection and love now belong to her. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
   * **my sister** - By calling her "sister," he is showing her purity.
   * **with one jewel of your necklace** - He is implying that she has many good qualities. He is saying only one quality of hers would have been enough to attract him to her.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **perfume** - a sweet smelling liquid made to put on the body in small amounts
  * **spice** - dried plants or seeds to add flavor in cooking 
  * **Your lips, my bride, drip honey; honey and milk are under your tongue** - He is not saying honey is actually dripping from her lips. This is a word picture to say how desirable she is to him. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **fragrance** - a pleasing smell
  * **garments** - "pieces of clothing"
  * **Lebanon** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:sng:03:08|3:09]].

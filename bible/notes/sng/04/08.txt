===== Translation Notes: =====

Solomon is inviting his wife to be close to him.

  * **Amana** - a mountain near Damascus (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Senir** - a mountain top in Lebanon
  * **Hermon** - a mountain in Palestine
  * **dens** - "hideaways" or "caves"

===== Translation Notes: =====

Solomon continues to praise his wife.

  * **neck is like the tower of David**  - This is a way to say her neck is long and strong. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **a thousand shields**- 1,000 shields (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **breasts are like two fawns**  - This is a way to say her breasts are soft and nice to touch as is the hair on a young deer.

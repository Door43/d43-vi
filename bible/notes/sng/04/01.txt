===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your eyes are doves** - Solomon uses the word "dove" as a way to express his love. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Your hair is like a flock of goats** - Goats are often dark in color and when they walk on the mountains their hair would look like the long waves in a woman's hair. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

Solomon continues to compliment and admire his wife.
  * **I will go to the mountain of myrrh and to the hill of frankincense** - He is referring to his wife's  breasts. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **my love**  - This is a sweet term of endearment, from Solomon to his wife.
  * **blemish** - "stain" or "scar"

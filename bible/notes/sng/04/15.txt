===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You are a garden spring, a well of fresh water,
streams flowing down** -These ideas are repeated to show how refreshing Solomon thinks his wife is. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **You are a garden spring** - Solomon is admiring his wife. He doesn't think she is a real spring. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * ** Awake, north wind; come, south wind; blow** - He talks to the wind as though it were a person. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) 
  * **blow on my garden so that its spices may give off their fragrance** - She is treating her body as though it is a garden. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **May my beloved come into his garden and eat some of its choice fruit** - She is letting Solomon know her body is his, and she is ready for him.

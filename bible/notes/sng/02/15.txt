===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Catch the jackals for us, the little jackals
that spoil vineyards**  - She wants him to stop anything that might spoil their marriage. 
    * **jackals** - fierce wild dogs
    * **in blossom**  - "in flower." Their marriage is good. They are greatly in love.

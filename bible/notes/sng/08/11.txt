===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Baal Hamon** - See how to [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]. 
  * **maintain** - "manage" or "protect"
  * **Solomon…silver for its fruit.**  ​- This desription of Solomon shows how wealthy he was. He had vineyards and workers to care for the fruit. The workmen had to pay him for the rental of the vineyards.
  * **My vineyard is my very own**  - The woman is comparing Solomon's wealth to her wealth.

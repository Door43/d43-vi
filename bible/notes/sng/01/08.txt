===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If you do not know...follow the tracks of my flock** - The man answers her question about finding him by explaining how the woman can find him easily. He is not trying to avoid her or deceive her because he loves her as much as she loves him.
  * **tracks** - marks of the hooves of the goats on the ground
  * **pasture your young goats near the shepherds' tents** - The man is explaining that he wants the woman to be close to him. He is not trying to hide anything from her.
  * **pasture your young goats** - "feed your young goats" AT: "let your young goats feed"

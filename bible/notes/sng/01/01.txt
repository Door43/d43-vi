===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[vi:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]
  * **The Song of Songs** - "The Best Song" or "The Most Excellent Song"
  * **which is Solomon’s** - "which Solomon made" or "which Solomon composed"
  * **Oh, that you would kiss me** - "Please kiss me"
  * **of your mouth** - "that you give with your mouth"
  * **Your anointing oils** - "The oils that you put on your body"
  * **have a delightful fragrance** - "smell wonderful"
  * **your name is like flowing perfume** - The woman realizes that her lover has a good reputation. AT: "your name is wonderful like the smell of oil that someone has poured out." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Take me with you** - "Bring me along with you."
  * **we will run** - "we will run after you." The word "we" refers to the young women of Jerusalem. The word "you" refers to the lover.
  * **about you** - "because of you"
  * **let me celebrate** - "let me praise"
  * **It is natural for the other women to adore you** - "All who loves you are right"
  * **adore** - "love"

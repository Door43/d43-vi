===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There are sixty queens, eighty concubines, and young women without number. My dove, my undefiled, is the only one** - Though Solomon has many women, she is the most important one. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * ** sixty queens, eighty concubines** - "60 queens, 80 concubines" (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **my undefiled** - "my pure one" or "my clean one"
  * **who bore her** - "who gave birth to her"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I went to the prophetess** -  It can be stated explicitly that Isaiah is married to the prophetess: "I slept with my wife, the prophetess." (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]] and [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the riches of Damascus and the plunder of Samaria will be carried away by the king of Assyria** - AT: "The king of Assyria will carry away all the treasures of Damascus and Samaria." (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

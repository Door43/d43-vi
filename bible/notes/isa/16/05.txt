===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A throne will be established in covenant faithfulness** - Here "throne" refers to the power to rule as king over the people. AT: "Yahweh will be faithful to the covenant and he will appoint a king." (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **and one from David's tent will faithfully sit there** - Here "David's tent" means the king will be a descendant of King David. AT: "and he will be a descendant of King David and he will be faithful"

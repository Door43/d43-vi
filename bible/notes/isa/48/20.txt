===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **his servant Jacob** - AT: "the people of Israel, his servants" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to the ends of the earth!** - to the farthest parts of the whole world (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

Matthew quotes a prophecy that the birth of Jesus would fulfill.

  * **what was spoken by the Lord through the prophet**  - This could be stated in the active voice as "what the Lord told the prophet Isaiah to write long ago." (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **Behold**  - Alternative translation: “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **the virgin will conceive and give birth to a son**  - This verse is a direct quotation from Isaiah [[:vi:bible:notes:isa:07:13|7:14]].

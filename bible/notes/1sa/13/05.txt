===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **thirty thousand chariots, six thousand men**  - 30,000 chariots, 6,000 men (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **troops as numerous as the sand on the seashore** - A group of soldiers so large that it was difficult to count them. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
    * **Michmash** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Bethaven**  - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

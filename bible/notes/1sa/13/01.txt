===== Translation Notes: =====

Samuel has renewed Saul's kingdom at Gilgal and Samuel has reminded the people to follow the Lord. 

    * **The rest of the soldiers he sent home** - "He sent the rest of the soldiers home"
    * **chose three thousand men** - "chose 3,000 men" (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **Two thousand were with him in Michmash** - "2,000 were with him in Mishmash" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Gibeah of Benjamin** - See how you translated "Gibeah" in [[vi:bible:notes:1sa:10:26|10:26]]

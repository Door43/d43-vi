===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cut off your strength**  - This is another way of saying cause the family's health to decline. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism|Euphemism]])
  * **in the prime of life**  - AT: "While they are young, vigorous and healthy men." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **he will become blind from weeping**  - AT: "he will cry so much that his eyes will be destroyed"

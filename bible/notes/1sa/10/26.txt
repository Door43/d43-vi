===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Gibeah**  - This is the name of a town. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **and with him went some strong men,**  - "and some strong men went with him,"
    * **whose hearts God had touched.**  - "because God gave them the desire to do this." (See: [[[[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **"How can this man save us?"**  - (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **These people despised Saul**  - "These people disliked Saul very much"

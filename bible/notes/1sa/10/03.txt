===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Tabor**  - This is the name of a place or possibly a mountain. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Three men going to God**  - "Three men on their way to worship God"
  * **one carrying three young goats,**  - "one carrying 3 young goats," (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **another carrying three loaves of bread,**  - "another carrying 3 loaves of bread," A loaf is an amount of bread that has been baked in a round or oblong shape.
  * **a skin of wine.** - Here "skin" refers to a pouch made of animal hide that holds wine.

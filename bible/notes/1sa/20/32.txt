===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then Saul threw his spear at him to kill him.**  - This was done at the new moon festival in front of other people.  This showed Jonathan that his father was very angry about Jonathan's friendship with David. Saul threw the spear at Jonathan because he was very angry at Jonathan.
  * **had dishonored him**  - "Him" refers to Jonathan.

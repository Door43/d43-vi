===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now the Philistines fought against Israel** - The battle happening here was the battle set up in [[:vi:bible:notes:1sa:29:10|29:11]].  Chapter 30 went onto a different story, and now the writer is coming back to the battle between the Israelites and Philistines.
  * **the Philistines fought against Israel** - "the Philistines fought against the people of Israel".
  * **Mount Gilboa**  - This is the name of a mountain (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]).
  * **closely pursued Saul and his sons** - "closely pursued Saul and three of this sons".
  * **Abinidab, and Malchishua** - These are names of men.
  * **The battle went heavily against Saul** - "Saul's enemies fought even harder against him"
  * **He was in severe pain because of them.** - "They caused him severe pain."

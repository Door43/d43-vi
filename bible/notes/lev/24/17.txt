===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
  * **And he who kills another man must certainly be put to death** - AT: "You must certainly put to death anyone who kills another person" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **life for life** - AT: "one life to replace the other life" (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])

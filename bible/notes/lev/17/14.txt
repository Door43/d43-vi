===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **is together, the same, the life is one with the blood** - "are the same. The life is one with the blood"
    * **You must not eat the blood of any animal** - "You may not eat the meat of an animal with the blood still in it" or "You may not consume the blood of any animal"
    * **Whoever eats it must be cut off** - AT: "You must remove from the people anyone who consumes blood" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
  * **to feed the fire** - AT: "to keep the fire burning" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he must wash** - Here "he" refers to the one making the offering.
  * **an offering made to me by fire**  - AT: "a burnt offering to me" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

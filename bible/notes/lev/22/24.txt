===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **from a foreigner's hand** - AT: "from a foreigner" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **as food presented to God** - God did not actually eat the sacrifices. Priests would offer the sacrifice on God's altar, and they would eat some of the meat. AT: "as a food offering to God" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

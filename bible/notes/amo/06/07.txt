===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Declaration of the Lord Yahweh** - Translate this as you did in [[:vi:bible:notes:amo:03:13|3:13]]. 
    * **I hate his fortresses** - "I hate the people of Israel because they trust in their fortresses, not in me, to protect them"
    * **fortresses** - walls built around cities to protect them from attackers (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ask Yahweh's will for me, **- AT: "Ask Yahweh for me what his will is," (See: Verbal noun)
  * **because of the words of the book**  - "according to the book"
  * ** For it is great, the anger of Yahweh that has been poured out on us. It is great, ** - AT: "For Yahweh's the anger towards us is great," (See: Parallelism) (See also: Metaphorical expression: "anger...poured out")

===== Translation Notes: =====
This is still about the king of the North.

  * **the Libyans and the Ethiopians** - "the people of Libya and Ethiopia." Libya is a country west of Egypt, and Ethiopia is a country south of Egypt. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you set your mind to understand** - "you determined to understand the vision"
  * **your words were heard** -  "God heard your words" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **prince** - This refers here to a spirit who has authority over a human nation. AT: "spirit prince"

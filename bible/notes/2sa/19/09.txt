===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **arguing**  - to give reasons for or against something
    * **hand of our enemies, hand of the Philistines**  - A figure of speech referring to the control and power of those named. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **why are we not talking**  - An emphatic way of saying "we should be talking". (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])

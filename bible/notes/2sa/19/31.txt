===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Barzillai, Rogelim**  - Translate the same way you did in [[vi:bible:notes:2sa:17:27]].
    * **Gileadite**  - A word indicating someone is from [[:vi:obe:other:gilead]].
    * **the Jordan**  - a short name for the [[vi:obe:other:jordanriver]].
    * **eighty years old**  - a measure of time living: 80 (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **furnished, provisions**  - to provide something necessary
    * **Mahanaim**  - Translate the same way you did in [[vi:bible:notes:2sa:02:08]].
    * **wealthy**  - having a great deal of money or assets

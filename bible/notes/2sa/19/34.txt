===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **distinguish**  - perceive a difference
    * **taste**  - a persons liking to flavors
    * **hear**  - to perceive with the ear a sound from someone or something
    * **singing**  - musical sounds with the mouth
    * **burden**  - a heavy load

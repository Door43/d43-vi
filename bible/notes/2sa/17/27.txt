===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Mahanaim, Shobi, Rabbah, Ammonites, Machir, Ammiel, Lo Debar, Barzillai, Gileadite, Rogelim**  - See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]
    * **and milk curds**  - Curdled milk can mean cheese in general or cottage-cheese in particular.   The vinegar contained in cottage-cheese is a preservative making this more practical than milk which would spoil quickly in the heat.

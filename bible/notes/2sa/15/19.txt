===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Ittai**  - The name of a man. (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Gittite**  - The name of a people group (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Why did you go you with us, too?**  - This is a [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] indicating that the king had not asked them to go along.   In the next sentence the king clarified his wishes for them to return home. "Us" refers to the king and his family. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **why should I make you wander all over with us?**  - This is also a [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] indicating the reason why.  See the UDB also for clarification.
    * **for you are a foreigner and an exile**  - See the UDB for additional clarification.   It could be that David feared that Ittai the Gittite might be placed in the precarious position of opposing his own people.

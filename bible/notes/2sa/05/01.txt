===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we are your flesh and bone **  - This phrase means "relative." AT:"we are related to you" or "we are of the same family." - (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **In the recent past** - This is historical information. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]]) 
  * **you** - Here this refers to David. (See: [[ vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_youformal]]) 
  * **shepherd my people Israel, ...ruler over Israel** -  These two phrase have similar meanings and are combined here for emphasis. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])

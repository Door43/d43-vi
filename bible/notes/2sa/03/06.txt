===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Rizpah... Aiah**  - These are female names. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Ishbosheth** - This is a male name, a son of Saul. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Why have you slept with my father’s concubine ** - Here the word "slept" is a polite way to say "had sex with." AT: "Why have you had sex with my father's concubine" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])

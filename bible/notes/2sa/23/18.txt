===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Abishai**  - Translate the same way you did in [[vi:bible:notes:2sa:02:18]].
    * **Zeruiah**  - Translate the same way you did in [[vi:bible:notes:2sa:02:12]].
    * **he**  - refers to Abishai (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **captain over the three**  - AT: leader in charge of the 3. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **three hundred men**  - a number of men, 300
    * **was he not even more famous**  - AT: "he was" without the question. This is [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] in question form. "Famous" is having many people talk about you in positive ways regularly.

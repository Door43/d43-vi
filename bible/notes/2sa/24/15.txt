===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a fixed time **- God has an appointed time to perform His will or Word and how much time it was to destroy men of Jerusalem
  * **from Dan to Beersheba **- location of all the tribes of Israel that were counted
  * **the angel reached out with his hand toward Jerusalem to destroy it, **- "the angel was given the order by God to destroy Jerusalem" [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]
  * **Yahweh changed his mind about the harm, **- "Yahweh stopped the evil that he was allowing the angel to do," [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]

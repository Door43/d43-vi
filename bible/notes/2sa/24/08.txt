===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they came back to Jerusalem at the end of nine months and twenty days. **- The author's note about the timeframe to take the census of the children of Israel. This only included the men.
  * **Then Joab reported the total of the count of the fighting men to the king. **- Joab told the total number of men ready for battle to the King.
  * **There were in Israel eight hundred thousand brave men who drew the sword, and the men of Judah were five hundred thousand men. **- The author's note about the split tribes and the census being divided to show how large the armies of Israel and Judah were.

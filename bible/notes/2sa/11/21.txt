===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Did not a woman cast an upper millstone on him from the wall, so that he died at Thebez? ?**  - This is a [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] stating a fact.  See also: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]].
    * **Why did you go so near the wall**  - This is a [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] implying that the army never should have gone so close to the wall.
    * **millstone**  - A stone used to crush grain to get the flour to make bread with.

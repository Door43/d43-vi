===== Translation Notes: =====
David continues to talk to Yahweh

  * **the promise that you made **  - the covenant that was made with David and Israel before him by God
  * **your servant and his family **  - This refers to David and the the nation Israel that made David their king (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the people** - This refers to the nation of Israel. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

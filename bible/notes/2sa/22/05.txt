===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **waves of death; waters of worthlessness; cords of Sheol; and snares of death**  - These are figures of speech that David is using to refer to his emotions and physical experiences. (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

    * **Worthlessness**  - a state of being unimportant or useless

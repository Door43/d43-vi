===== Translation Notes: =====

These verses are describing David's perception of God's reaction & response to his cry for help from his enemies. (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

    * **shook and trembled**  - moving with quick, short, involuntary movements from fear or anxiety
    * **smoke**  - gas given  off by burning items
    * **coals**  - black or dark brown rocks, found in the earth, that can be used for fuel

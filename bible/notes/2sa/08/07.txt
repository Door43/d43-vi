===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **golden shields** - These could be small circular shields or quivers for arrows or a case for bows. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **were on Hadadezer's servants** - This is a passive sentence. AT:"which the Hadadezer's servants had carried." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Betah and Berothai**  - These are the names of places. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Joab said,** - Here Joab is addressing "some of Israel's best fighters" from verse 9: [[:vi:bible:notes:2sa:10:09]]
    * **Be strong, and let us**  - Here the "us" is inclusive since Joab is addressing **???** on a matter of mutual benefit.   See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]]
    * **for Yahweh will do what is good for his purpose** - David was confident in the Word of Yahweh/God which promised Yahweh/God would protect and stand up for the people of Israel.

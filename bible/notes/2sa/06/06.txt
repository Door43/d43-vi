===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Nacon**  - This is the male name of a person. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **anger of Yahweh burned** - Here the "anger of Yahweh" is compared to fire. AT: "Yahweh quickly became very angry like a fire would quickly burn." (See:  [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **presumption**  - Uzzah was too bold in his action that was not permitted for people not of the tribe of Levi.

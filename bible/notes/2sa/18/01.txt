===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **soldiers**  - men or women who serve in an army
  * **captain**  - a person who is at the head or in authority
  * **hundreds**  - a number unit depicting amount
  * **one third**  - one of three pieces of a whole
  * **Abishai, Zeruiah, Ittai, Gittite**  - names of people or people group (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still talking to the people of Israel.

  * **He humbled you** - "Yahweh made you humble"
  * **fed you with manna** - "gave you manna to eat"
  * **people do not live by bread only** - Bread is a symbol of all food. AT: "food is not the only thing people need to live." (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **it is by everything that proceeds out of the mouth of Yahweh that people live** - The mouth is a symbol for the things that Yahweh says. AT: "people must obey the commands of Yahweh so they can live" or "people must do what Yahweh tells them to do so they can live." (See: [[vi:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:synecdoche]])

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **a mill or an upper millstone**  - The Israelites made flour by grinding grain between two heavy discs of stone, a lower immobile one and an upper one which turned in a circle.
    * **he would be taking a man's livelihood**  - That is, he would be taking from the man what he needed to prepare food for his family from day to day. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **poor and needy**  - These two words mean basically the same thing. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **within your city gates**  - This means that they live in your town, which is protected by gates.
    * **each day you must give him his wage**  - This means that they should pay their workers at the end of every single day; if not, the workers would not have money to buy food for that day.
    * **the sun must not go down on this unsettled matter**  - AT: "You must pay a man his wages before sundown each day." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **cry out against you to Yahweh**  - AT: "pray to Yahweh with emotion and ask him to punish you for what you have done."

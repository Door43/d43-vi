===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **kidnapping**  - To kidnap means to take someone away, using physical force.
    * **any of his brothers of the people of Israel**  - This refers to any Israelite, not just one's biological brother.
    * **that thief must die**  - This means that other Israelites should kill him as punishment for his crime.

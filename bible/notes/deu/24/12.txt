===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **cloak**  - This is a large rectangular piece of cloth, which the person can wrap around himself to keep warm at night.
    * **so that he may sleep in his cloak and bless you**  - AT: "So that he will be able to keep himself warm through the night and will be grateful to you for not taking it away from him."
    * **it will be righteousness for you before Yahweh your God.**  - AT: "Yahweh your God will approve of the way you handled this matter."

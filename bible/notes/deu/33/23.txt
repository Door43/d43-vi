===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues blessing each tribe of Israel.

  * **satisfied with favor, and full of the blessing of Yahweh** - "whom Yahweh has blessed and given many good things"
  * **the land to the west and south** - This refers to the land around the Lake Galilee. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])

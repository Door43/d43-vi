===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **the people** - "the people of Israel"
    * **all his holy people are in your hand**  - The pronouns "his" and "your" refer to Yahweh.  The phrase "in your hand" refers to Yahweh's power and protection.  AT: "All your people are in your power" or "Yahweh protects all his holy people" (See:[[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **an inheritance** - "a possession" or "a precious possession"

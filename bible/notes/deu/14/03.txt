===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must not eat any disgusting thing** - The people of Israel were not supposed to eat anything that God said was unclean.
  * **the gazelle** - The gazelle is a type of antelope. See how you translated these in [[vi:bible:notes:deu:12:15|12:15]].
  * **the roebuck** - This is a type of deer. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **the ibex** - a type of mountain goat.

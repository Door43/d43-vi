===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the work of your hand**  - "in all the work you do" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in the fruit of your body**  - AT: "in children" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in the fruit of your cattle**  - AT: "in calves"
  * **in the fruit of your ground** - AT: "in crops"
  * **that are written** - AT: "that I have written" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **all your heart and all your soul** - AT: "with complete commitment" or "completely" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])

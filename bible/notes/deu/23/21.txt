===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **you must not be slow in fulfilling it**  - "you must not take a long time to fulfill the vow"
  * **for Yahweh your God will surely require it of you**  - AT: "because Yahweh your God will blame you and punish you if you do not fulfill your vow." ([[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **But if you will refrain from making a vow, it will be no sin in you**  - AT: "But, if you do not make a vow, you will not sin because you will not have a vow to fulfill." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **That which has gone out from your lips**  -  AT: "Anything that you solemnly promise." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] )
  * **according as you have vowed to Yahweh your God**  - "whatever you have vowed to Yahweh your God that you will do"
  * **anything that you have freely promised with your mouth**  -  AT: "anything that you have voluntarily promised with your words." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])

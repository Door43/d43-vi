===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel. 
 
  * **goes into her**  - AT: "has sexual relations with her." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **and then accuses her of shameful things**  - AT: "and then accuses her of having sex before marriage."
  * **and puts a bad reputation on her**  - "and gives her a bad reputation"
  * **but when I came near to her**  - AT: "but when I had sex with her." 
  * **I found no proof of virginity in her** - "I did not find proof that she was a virgin"

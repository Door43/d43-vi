===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **If a man is found**  - AT: "If someone finds a man." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **lying with a woman**  - AT: "having sex with a woman." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **In this manner**  - "In this way"
  * **you must eradicate the evil**  - "you must get rid of the evil" or "you must destroy the evil"

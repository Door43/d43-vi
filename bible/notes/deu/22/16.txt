===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **he has accused her of shameful things**  - AT: "he has accused her of having sex before their marriage." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **I did not find in your daughter the proof of virginity**  - "I did not find proof that your daughter was a virgin"
  * **yet this is the proof of my daughter’s virginity**  - "but here is the proof that my daughter was a virgin"
  * **And then they will spread the garment out**  - "And then the father and mother will show the clothing to the elders." The clothing had a bloodstain from the daughter's wedding night, which showed that her wedding night was the first time she had sex. AT: "And then the mother and father will show the clothing with the bloodstain to the elders as proof of her virginity." (See:  [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **within your city gates**  - AT: “within your towns”  (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **fatherless** - Children whose parents have both died and do not have relatives to care for them.
  * **widows** - a true widow is a woman who's husband has died and has no children to care for her in her old age.
  * **call to mind** - AT: "remember" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

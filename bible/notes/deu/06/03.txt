===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **listen to them** -  "listen to Yahweh's commandments, statutes and decrees". To "listen" in this case is to obey. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **a land flowing with milk and honey** - "a land where plenty of milk and honey flow." This means that the land will be rich and productive, so there will be enough food for everyone. AT: "a land that is excellent for cattle and farming" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

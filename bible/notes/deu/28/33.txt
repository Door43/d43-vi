===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a nation** - AT: "people from a nation" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you will always be oppressed and crushed** - The words "oppressed" and "crushed" mean basically the same thing. AT: "they will always oppress and crush you" or "they will oppress you continually" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **you will become insane by what you have to see happen**  - AT: "what you see will drive you mad"
  * **from which you cannot be cured** - AT: "which no one will be able to cure"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cause your enemies who rise up against you to be struck down before you** - AT: "cause you to defeat the armies that attack you" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **but will flee before you seven ways** - "but will scatter before you in various directions." Their enemies will run away in fear and panic. 
  * **Yahweh will command the blessing to come on you in your barns** - AT: "Yahweh will bless your crops so you have plenty to store in your barns" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and in all that you put your hand to**- AT: "in and everything you do"

    * **These tribes** - AT: "the people from these tribes" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **Mount Gerizim** - This was a small mountain north of Mount Ebal. See how you translated it in [[vi:bible:notes:deu:11:29|11:29]].
    * **Joseph** - This combines the tribes of Ephraim and Manasseh who were descendants of Joseph.

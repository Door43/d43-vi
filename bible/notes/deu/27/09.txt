===== Translation Notes: =====

 
    * **obey the voice of Yahweh your God** - AT: "obey what Yahweh your God says" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **I am commanding** - Here "I" refers to Moses. The Levites are standing in agreement with Moses, but he is the only one speaking.

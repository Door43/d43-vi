===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Notice that I am not speaking to your children**  - The only children present are those who were born in the wilderness. All the adults now listening to Moses were children at the time their parents rebelled against God in the desert.
  * **his mighty hand**  - AT: "his strength" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **in the midst of Egypt**  - "in Egypt"

===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **possess the land** - To take ownership of the land.
  * **a land flowing with milk and honey** - a very fertile land. See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:deu:06:03|6:3]].

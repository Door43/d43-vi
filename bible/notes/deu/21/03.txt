===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **borne the yoke**  - "worn a yoke"
  * **running water**  - "flowing water." a stream that doesn't dry up seasonally. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **a valley that has been neither plowed nor sown**  - AT: "a valley where nobody has made a garden." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **plow**  - a tool that farmers use to break up the dirt in a field so they can plant seeds.

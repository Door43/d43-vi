===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There were four of Kohath's sons** - AT: "Kohath had 4 sons" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Kohath … Amram, Izhar, Hebron … Uzziel** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Aaron and his descendants were chosen on a permanent basis … forever** - AT: "Moses chose Aaron and his descendants to permanently … forever" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **But as for Moses the man of God, his sons were considered to be Levites** - AT: "Moses, the man of God, and his sons were part of the Levite clan"

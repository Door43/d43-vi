===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So all Israel was recorded in genealogies. They were recorded in the Book of the Kings of Israel**  - This refers to the written family histories of all the descendants of Jacob (the 12 tribes) prior to the exile of Judah to Babylon. Verse 1 in this chapter is a conclusion to the genealogies in chapters 2 through 8. AT: "The Book of the Kings of Israel contained the names and family history of all the people of Israel" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **As for Judah, it was carried away in exile to Babylon** AT: "The Babylonians took most of the people of Judah away in exile to Babylon" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

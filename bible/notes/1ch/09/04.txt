===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Uthai ... Jeuel**  -  These are names of men. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shilonites**  - This is the name of a people group descended from Shelah.  It is a spelling variation for the Shelanites.
  * **Their descendants numbered 690** - AT: "There were six hundred ninety descendants" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

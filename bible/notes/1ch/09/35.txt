===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Gibeon ... Mikloth**  - These are the names of people. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **lived in Gibeon **  - In this phrase, Gibeon is the name of a city.
  * Note:  1 Chronicles 9:35-44 is almost identical to 1 Chronicles 8:29-38, other than a few spelling variations.  These two passages record genealogical records for King Saul's family.

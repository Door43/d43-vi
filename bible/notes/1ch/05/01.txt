===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **now Reuben** - The word "now" is used here the change from the story to background information about Reuben. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **but his birthright was given to the sons of Joseph son of Israel** - AT: "but Israel gave Reuben's birthright to the sons of Joseph, another of Israel's sons" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Reuben had defiled his father’s couch** - This is a polite way to speak about Reuben having sex with father's secondary wife. (See:[[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]]) Reuben's secondary wife is referred to as his couch because that is the place a man and wife would have sex. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **So he is not recorded  as being the oldest** - AT: "So the family history does not list Reuben as the oldest son" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Hanoch...Pallu...Hezron...Carmi**  - These are the names of men. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

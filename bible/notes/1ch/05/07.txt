===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **His brothers by their clans are the following, listed in their geneological records** - AT: Joel's brothers and their families, whose names were in the family history record, are the following" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **genealogical record** - This shows how the members of the same family are related to each other.
  * **Jeiel, Zechariah, Bela, Azaz, Shema** - These are names of men. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Aroer, Nebo, Baal Meon** - These are names of cities in Gilead.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Azmaveth ... Zabdi** - These are names of men. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in charge of** - AT: "responsible for guarding"
  * **fortified towers ** - "strong towers"
  * **plow** - to dig into or break up the dirt before planting
  * **Shepham** - This is the name of a place.
  * **cellar**  - the part of a building that is below the ground and used to store wine

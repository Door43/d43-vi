===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will be a father to him…**  - God is speaking. Nathan will give this message to King David.
  * “I” and "my" refer to God.
  * "Him," "his" and "he" - in vs. 13-14 refer to David's son, Solomon,  who will be born later.
  * “You” refers to David.
  * **in my kingdom forever, and his throne will be established forever** - (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]) 
  * **reported** - "told"

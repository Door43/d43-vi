===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The father of Gibeon, Jeiel, whose wife’s name was Maacah, lived in Gibeon**  - AT: "Jeiel, Gibeon's father, lived in Gibeon. His wife's name was Maacah"
  * **Gibeon** - This is the name of a man, and the name of a place named after that man. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jeiel ... Zecher** - These are names of people.  
  * **His firstborn** - "Jeiel's firstborn"

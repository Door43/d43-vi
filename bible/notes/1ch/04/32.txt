===== Important Terms: =====



===== Translation Notes: =====
Places where the descendants of Simeon lived continue to be recorded.

  * **Etam...Ain...Rimmon...Tochen...Ashan** - These are the names of villages. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **outlying** - far from a main population.
  * **Baal** - This is the name of a city.

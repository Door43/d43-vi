===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Eleazar son of Dodo, the Ahohite** - Eleazar and Dodo are men.  Ahohite is name for a descendant of Ahoh.  (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **three mighty men** - "3 mighty men"  (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Pasdammin** - This is the name of a place.
  * **barley** - a type of grain used to make food
  * **cut down** - "defeated"

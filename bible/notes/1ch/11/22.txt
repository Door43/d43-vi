===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Benaiah ... Jehoiada ... Ariel** - These are names of men. (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **feats** - acts that show courage, strength, or skill
  * **two sons** - "2 sons"  (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **while it was snowing** - AT: "during a snow storm"
  * **five cubits** - "5 cubits" or "over 2 meters" (See: [[:vi:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]])
  * **he went down to him** - AT: "Benaiah met the Egyptian"
  * **weaver's beam** - a large, heavy beam used to support the structure of a weaver's loom  AT:  "weaver's spindle" or "weaver's rod" (UDB)

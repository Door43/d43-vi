===== Translation Notes: =====

This is the completion of the listing of the names in order of the men chosen by lot which began in [[:vi:bible:notes:1ch:24:07|1 Chronicles 24:7]].

  * **seventeenth ... twenty-fourth** - This shows the order in which the leaders were chosen by the casting of lots. AT: “number 17…number 18…number 19…number 20…number 21…number 22…number 23…number 24” (See: Ordinal Numbers and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Hezir ... Maaziah** - These are names of men. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

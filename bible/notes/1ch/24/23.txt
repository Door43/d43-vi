===== Translation Notes: =====

This is a continuation of the list of names of the other sons of Levi that began in 1 Chronicles 24:20.

  * **Hebron's descendants included Jeriah the oldest, Amariah the second, Jahaziel the third, Jekameam the fourth.** - See how you translated the names and numbers in [[:vi:bible:notes:1ch:23:19|1 Chronicles 23:19]].
  * **Hebron...Uzziel** - Both of these men were sons of Kohath.  (UDB)
  * **Uzziel...Micah...Isshiah** - See how you translated these names in [[:vi:bible:notes:1ch:23:19|1 Chronicles 23:20]].
  * **Shamir...Zechariah** - These are the names of men. (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **first ... eighth** - This shows the order in which the leaders were chosen by the casting of lots. AT: "number 1...number 2...number 3...number 4...number 5...number 6...number 7...number 8" or substitute "original" for "first" and "next" for each of the others as in UDB.  (See: Ordinal Numbers and [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Jehoiarib ... Hakkoz** - These are the names of men. (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ezer ... Machbannai** - These are the names of men.  (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **second ... eleventh** - This is listing these men in order of leadership by ordinal numbers. AT: "2nd...3rd...4th...5th...6th...7th...8th...9th...10th...11th"  (See: Ordinal Numbers and [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Eliel** - See how you translated this in [[:vi:bible:notes:1ch:11:45|1 Chronicles 11:46]].
  * **Johanan** - See how you translated this in [[:vi:bible:notes:1ch:12:03]].

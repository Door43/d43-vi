===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The least led a hundred** - This means that the smallest group of the army that a leader lead was 100. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]) 
  * **the greatest lead a thousand** - This means that the largest group of the army that a leader lead was 1,000.
  * **first month** - AT: "month number 1"

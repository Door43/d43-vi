===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Gadites** - This is the name of the tribe of Gad's descendants. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **faces were as fierce as the faces of lions** - The Gadites are compared to lions to show how brave they were and and how their enemies feared them. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **they were as swift as gazelles**  - The Gadites are compared to gazelles to show that they could run and climb very fast. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **gazelle** - a small animal that is graceful and fast

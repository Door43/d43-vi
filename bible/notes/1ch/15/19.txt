===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Heman, Asaph, and Ethan … Zechariah, Aziel, Shemiramoth, Jehiel, Unni, Eliab, Maaseiah, Benaiah … Mattithiah, Eliphelehu, Mikneiah, Obed Edom, Jeiel, and Azaziah**  - These are names of people. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Alamoth ... Sheminith**  - The meaning of these words is not clear but may refer to a style of music or instruments with high or low sounds.
  * **led the way** - "led the other musicians"

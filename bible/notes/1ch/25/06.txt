===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jeduthun and Heman** - See how you translated these in [[:vi:bible:notes:1ch:16:40|1 Chronicles 16:41]].
  * **They and their brothers** - "They" refers to Asaph, Heman, and Jeduthun; "their brothers" refers to the descendants of these three men.
  * **numbered 288** - " were two hundred eighty eight men" (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]) 
  * **all alike, the young as well as the old, the teacher as the student** - This is describing the extremes. AT: "every person" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pedaiah...Shimei...Meshullam...Hananiah...Hashubah...Ohel...Berechiah...Hasadiah...Jushab Hesed...Pelatiah...Jeshaiah...Rephaiah...Arnan...Shecaniah** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shelomith** - This is the name of a woman.
  * **Obadiah** - This man has the same name as the prophet Obadiah but is a different person.

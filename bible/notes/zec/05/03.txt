===== Translation Notes: =====
The angel continues to speak with Zechariah.

  * **goes out over the face of**- This phrase refers to the curse like a cloud that spreads to cover the face of the land.  The word "face" refers to the surface of the land like a person's face is on the surface of their head. AT: "effects" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **based on what it says on the one side**  - "based on what the scroll has written on one side"
  * **what it says on the other side** - "what the scroll has written on the other side"
  * **according to their words**  - The phrase "their words" refers to what was said in their oath. AT: "according to what they said" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **'I will send it out**  - The word "it" refers to the curse.
  * **the house** - This refers to the man's family and everything they own. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **of the one that swears** - "of the man that swears"
  * **timber** - wood used for building

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Machir** - This is the name of Joseph's grandson. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **They were "born on his knees"** - This expression meant that they received special inheritance rights. AT: "Joseph gave them special inheritance rights." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **returned**  - The implied information about where he was returning to can be made explicit: "returned to where he was living." (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Melchizedek, king of Salem** - This is the first time this king is mentioned.
  * **bread and wine** - People commonly ate bread and wine. See how you translated "bread" in [[vi:bible:notes:gen:03:17|3:19]] and "wine" in [[vi:bible:notes:gen:09:20|9:21]].

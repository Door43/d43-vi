  * **this second demand** - AT: "what you have demanded the second time" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **May the gods do so to me and more also** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:1ki:19:01|19:2]].
  * **if even the ashes of Samaria will be enough for all the people who follow me to have a handful each** - Benhadad is saying that his army will totally destroy everything in Samaria. See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

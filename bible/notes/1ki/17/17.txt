===== Translation Notes: =====

 * 
  * **no more breath left in him** - AT: "he stopped breathing" or "he died" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **What have I to do with you** - This could be translated as "What do we have in common?" or "I have nothing to do with you!" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **man of God** - The phrase "man of God" is another title for a prophet.

  * **gave him a lamp in Jerusalem** - AT: "gave to David a descendant to rule in Jerusalem as a reminder of his covenant with David's family" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **what was right in his eyes** - AT: "what Yahweh considered to be right" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

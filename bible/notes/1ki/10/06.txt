===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your words and your wisdom** - Here the words "words" and "wisdom" mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]).
  * **my eyes have seen it** - AT: "I have seen it" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Not half was told me** - AT: "People told me very little" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

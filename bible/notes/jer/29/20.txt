===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Kolaiah, Maaseiah **- These refer to people.  (See: Translate Names)
  * **See**  - AT: “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you”.
  * **I am about to hand them over**- A phrase such as “I am about to hand them over” could be translated as, “I am allowing their enemies to conquer them”. (See: Idiom)
  * **into the hand of Nebuchadnezzar **- This phrase means that Ahab and Zedekiah will be given to the enemy, Nebuchadnezzar. (See: Metonymy)

===== Translation Notes: =====

Jeremiah speaks to the captive Israelites.

  * **throne of David**  - David is not king at this time. This refers to the current king.
  * **See **- "Look, listen, pay attention"
  * **For I will make them like rotten figs that are too bad to be eaten **- Yahweh is telling them how bad their life will be.  (See: Simile)

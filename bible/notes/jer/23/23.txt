===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Yahweh's declaration** - "what Yahweh has declared" or "what Yahweh has said"    
    * **Am I only a God nearby... and not also far away?**  - Yahweh is emphasizing the point that he is both close and far away. AT : I am a God that is both near and far away. (See [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Can anyone hide in a secret place so I cannot see him?**  - This question emphasizes the point that Yahweh can see everything and there is nothing that is hidden from his sight. (See : [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Do I not fill the heavens and the earth?**  - Yahweh is emphasizing that he is everywhere, both in the heavens and the earth. (See : [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])

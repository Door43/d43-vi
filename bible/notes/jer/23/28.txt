===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **What does straw have to do with grain?**  - This phrase emphasizes the difference between straw and grain as a way to point out the difference between Yahweh's message and the messages of the false prophets. (See : [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])    
    * **Is not my word like fire? and like a hammer smashing the rock?**  - These questions emphasize the characteristics of true messages from Yahweh. It compares the effects of Yahweh's word on those who hear it to a fire and a hammer. (See : [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]], [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])

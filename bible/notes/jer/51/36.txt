===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore **- This begins Yahweh's reply to Jerusalem's request in verses 34 and 35.
  * **springs run dry.**  - Water will no longer come out of the springs.
  * **heaps of rubble,**  - This refers to the buildings becoming piles of trash.
  * **den of jackals,**- This means wild dogs will make their home where the city was.
  * **horror**  - This means a very strong feeling of fear.
  * **object of hissing, **- This means Babylon will be mocked.
  * **inhabitants**  - This means people who live in a certain place.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The daughter of Babylon is like a threshing floor **- Babylon is being compared to a threshing floor. It means that there will be a time of great pressure coming upon Babylon. (See: Simile)
  * **time of harvest will come to her**  - This means that Babylon's riches will be taken by her enemies. (See: Metaphor)

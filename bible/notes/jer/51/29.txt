===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **anguish **- Babylon will be in great pain.
  * **For the land will shake and be in anguish - **  This phrase compares how the land will shake like an earthquake during the enemy attack. (See: Metaphor)
  * **where there is no inhabitant **- There will be no one left living in Babylon after the attack.

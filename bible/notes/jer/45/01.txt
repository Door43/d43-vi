===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Baruch ... Neriah** - These are the names of men. (See: Translate Names)
  * **Baruch**  - He was Jeremiah's scribe or secretary.
  * **at Jeremiah's dictation **- Baruch wrote down the words that Jeremiah said.
  * **in the fourth year of Jehoiakim** - AT: "18 years before the Babylonians destroyed Jerusalem, during the time of Jehoiakim" (See: Background Info and biblical time)
  * **It said **- AT: "Jeremiah's message to Baruch said"
  * **Woe is me** - AT: "I am so miserable" (See: Idiom)
  * **added agony to my pain**  - AT: "caused me great pain"
  * **My groaning has wearied me** - AT: "I'm tired from crying" (See: Abstract Nouns)

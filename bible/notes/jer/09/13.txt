===== Translation Notes: =====

Jeremiah has just pictured the their crying and singing sad songs because Yahweh has ruined the land.

  * **abandoned** - left behind, forgotten
  * **listen to my voice** - They heard God's voice through hearing his prophets' words. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **or walk by it** - AT: "or follow my words" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **stubborn hearts** - "rebellious thoughts" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

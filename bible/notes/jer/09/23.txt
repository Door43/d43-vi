===== Translation Notes: =====

Jeremiah has just told them that many people of Judah will die.

  * **Yahweh says this: Do not let the wise man take pride…in his riches **  - He repeats ideas with a similar phrase. See: Parallelism)
  * **warrior **- "man who fights and hunts"
  * **wealthy man**  - AT: "man who has a lot of resources"
  * **insight**  - "understanding"

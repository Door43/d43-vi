===== Translation Notes: =====

Yaweh has just reminded people not to be proud about their wisdom, and riches, but to be pleased that they know God.

  * **when I will punish all the circumcised who are such only in their body**  - Circumcision here refers figuratively to those who have not been circumcised spiritually, who do not have a relationship with God.

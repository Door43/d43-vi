===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh has just reminded them of their leaving Yahweh's Law and of their idol worship.

  * **wormwood** - "poisonous plant"
  * **scatter them** - "spread them out"
  * **I will send out a sword** - The sword refers to an army of soldiers. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

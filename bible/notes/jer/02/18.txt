===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So now, why take the road to Egypt and drink the waters of Shihor? Why take the road to Assyria and drink the waters of the Euphrates River?**  - AT: "You should not travel to Shihor to make an alliance with the rulers of Egypt. You should not travel to the Euphrates River to make an alliance with Assyria! (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Shihor** - the southwestern boundary of Canaan, a dry river bed
  * **Your wickedness rebukes you, and your faithlessness punishes you.**  - AT: "Because you have been wicked and unfaithful, I will punish you." (See:[[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **dread** - great fear

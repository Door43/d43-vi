===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues to lament the sins of his people through Jeremiah.

  * **My sorrow has no end** - Yahweh's sorrow has no end.
  * **daughter of my people** - Yahweh lovingly refers to Israel as a daughter. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **Is not Yahweh in Zion**  - Jerusalem is referred to as Zion. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **Is not Yahweh in Zion? Or is not her king in her?**  - Two parallel questions that express a hope that God is with them. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Why then would they offend me**  - Yahweh is speaking.

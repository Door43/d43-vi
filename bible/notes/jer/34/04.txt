===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will not die by the sword. You will die in peace** - This means he will not have a violent death. (See: Metonymy)
  * **as in the funeral burning of your ancestors**  - "people will burn incense to honor you as they did for your ancestors" (UDB)
  * **They will lament for you ** - AT: "They will mourn your death"
  * **Now I have spoken. This is Yahweh’s declaration** - "I, Yahweh, promise that will happen." (UDB)

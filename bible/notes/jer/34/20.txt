===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **seeking their lives**  - "seeking to kill them"
    * **beasts**  - "animals"
    * **and will bring them back**  - "and will bring the Babylonian army back"
    * **inhabitants**  - Inhabitants are people who live in a certain place.

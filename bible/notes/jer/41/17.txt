===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Geruth Chimham**  - This is the name of a place. (See: Translate Names)
    * **because of the Chaldeans** - "because of the Babylonians"
    * **They were afraid of them since**  - AT: "They were afraid of what the Babylonians would do when they heard that"
    * **in charge of the land**  - "in charge of the land of Judah"

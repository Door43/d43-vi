===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to talk to Jeremiah. He is telling Jeremiah what to say to the king of Judah and the king's mother. 

    * **Lift up your eyes**  - See how you translated this in note [[:vi:bible:notes:jer:03:01|03:02]]
    * **Where is the flock he gave to you, the flock that was so beautiful to you?**  - Yahweh is asking the king of Judah where his people have gone.  (See:  [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **What will you say when God places over you those whom you had taught to be your friends?**  - Yahweh is asking the king what he will say when he has to serve former friends.  (See:  [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Are these not the beginnings of the labor pains that will seize you just like a woman in childbirth?**  - Yahweh is telling the king his capture is just the beginning of the pain he will go through.  (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])

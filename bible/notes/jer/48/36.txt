===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **my heart is lamenting for Moab like a flute** - Jeremiah was comparing his feeling to a sad song. (See: Simile) 
    * **my heart is lamenting**  - The word "heart" refers to the deep emotions felt by Jeremiah. AT: "I am deeply sad for". (See: Synecdoche)
    * **Kir Heres**  - This is the name of the place where captives from Moab were held. (See: How to Translate Names)
    * **For every head is bald and every beard shaved; incisions are on every hand, and sackcloth is around their waists** - These are all things that the people of Moab did when they were mourning or expressing deep sorrow. 
    * **incisions**  - cuts made on the skin

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Heshbon** - This is the name of the royal city of the king of Moab.  (see: How to Translate Names)
    * **Elealeh, Jahaz, Zoar, Horonaim' and  Eglath Shelishiyah**  - These are all names of other cities in Moab.  (See: How to Translate Names)
    * **Nimrim**  - This is the name of a stream or river near the Dead Sea.  (See: How to Translate Names)
    * **this is Yahweh’s declaration**  - See how you  translated this in 1:8.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Make him drunk -**  The judgments which God sends upon Moab shall allow the country to be scorned by his enemies; just as a drunken man is the object of menâ€™s laughter and mocking. (See: Metaphor)
  * **Now Moab claps his hands in disgust at his own vomit, so he also has become an object for laughter**. - God continues to compare Moab to a drunken man. (See: Metaphor)
  * **For did Israel not become an object for laughter to you? - **.**3)**  Yahweh used this question to rebuke Moab for mocking Israel. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **Was he found among thieves, that you shook your head at him as often as you spoke about him?**  Yahweh continues to rebuke Moab for mocking Israel. Israel had been guilty before God, but Israel was guiltless towards Moab. (See: Rhetorical Question)

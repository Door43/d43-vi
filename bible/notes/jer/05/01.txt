===== Translation Notes: =====
See: [[vi:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]\\
Jeremiah is speaking to the people of Jerusalem.
  * **Rush about through** - "Hurry around in"
  * **then look and think about this** - "look and find out"
  * **city squares** - broad and open places in a city where people can gather
  * **If you can find** - "whether you can find one person"
  * **or anyone** - "whether there is one person who"
  * **is acting justly** - "does what is just"
  * **seeking the truth** - "seeks to be faithful" or "seeks to be honest"
  * **Even if they** - The word "they" refers to the people of Jerusalem.
  * **Yahweh lives** - People used this phrase when making an solemn oath in order to ask Yahweh to punish them if they broke the oath. See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:jer:04:01|4:2]]. 
  * **do your eyes not look for truth** - Jeremiah uses this question to emphasize that what Yahweh really wants is faithfulness. AT: "you want people to be faithful." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **You strike the people, but they do not feel pain.** - AT: "You punish the people, but they do not pay attention." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **have completely defeated** - "have completely destroyed"
  * **to receive discipline** - "to learn their lesson"
  * **They make their faces harder than cliff sides** - AT: "They became very stubborn."

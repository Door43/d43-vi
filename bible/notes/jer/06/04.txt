===== Translation Notes: =====

A king from the attacking army is speaking to the men under his control.
  * **Attack her in Yahweh's name** - "Sanctify war against her." The attacking army tries guarantee that their gods will help them during the war by performing ceremonies and making sacrifices. AT: "Prepare for war by purifying yourselves and sacrificing to the gods." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Arise** - "Get up"
  * **at noon** - AT: "at noon even though it is very hot."
  * **the daylight is fading away** - "the day is ending"
  * **are falling** - "are stretching out"
  * **at night** - AT: "during the night even though it is dark."
  * **her fortresses**  - "the strong buildings of Jerusalem"

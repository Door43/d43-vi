===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Cut her trees** - "Cut down the trees." Yahweh is speaking to the army that will attack Jerusalem.
  * **siegeworks** - "siege mounds." These are mounds of dirt that will allow the enemy to attack the walls of Jerusalem.
  * **because it is filled with oppression** - "because its people are always oppressing each other"
  * **Just as a well keeps its water cool, so this city keeps its wickedness** - A well is supposed to keep its water cool so that is good water does not go bad. Like the well, Jerusalem has preserved its wickedness, even when Yahweh punishes it, as though it were precious water. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]])
  * **Violence and disorder are heard in her** - AT: "I hear violence and robbery in her." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Suffering and plague are continually before me** - AT: "I constantly see sickness and suffering." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Accept discipline, Jerusalem** - Yahweh speaks to Jerusalem as though she was a person. AT: "Learn from your punishment, you people of Jerusalem." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_Apostrophe]], [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_Personification]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_Metonymy]])
  * **an uninhabited land** - "a land that has no people living in it"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **by the hand of Jeremiah **- God spoke through the action of Jeremiah.  (See: Synecdoche)
  * **cause them to listen…listen**  - This phrase is repeated for emphasis. (See: Parallelism)
  * **Bel, Merodach **- These are the two names for the same god of Babylon. (See: How to Translate Names)
  * **Bel is made ashamed. Merodach is dismayed. Its idols are put to shame; its images are dismayed. - **  (See: Parallelism)

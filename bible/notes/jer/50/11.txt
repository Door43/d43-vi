===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stamping**  - See how you translated "stamping" in Jeremiah 47:3.
  * **mother**  - The word "mother" is a figure of speech. (See: Metaphor)
  * **a wilderness, a dry land, and a desert.**  - These words are used for emphasis (See: Parallelism)
  * **shudder **- to shake violently due to fear

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **join themselves to Yahweh**  - This is figurative language used to explain a spiritual connection to Yahweh. (See: Idiom)
  * **arise against it**  - The word "it" refers to Babylon.
  * **her land**  - The word "her" refers to Babylon.
  * **live in it**  - The word "it" refers to the land of Babylon.
  * **They will ask…They will go**  - The words "They" both refer to people of Israel or people of Judah.
  * **not be broken**  - This implies that the covenant will be obeyed forever.

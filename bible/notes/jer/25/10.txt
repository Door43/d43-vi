===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **joy and celebration**  - "Joy" and "celebration" mean the same thing and are included for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **millstones **- two large, round stones used to grind grain
  * **a desolation and a horror **-" Desolation" and "horror" mean the same thing and are included for emphasis. (See: Doublet)

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **wine of fury**  - Yahweh refers to an unpleasant event about to happen (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
    * **make all the nations to which I am sending you drink it**  - to make the nations drink it means to make them experience it. (See:[[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **they will stumble about and rant madly**  - those experiencing the consequences of Yahweh's severe punishment will appear like they are crazy people (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

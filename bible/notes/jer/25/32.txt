===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will on that day extend from one end of the earth to the other**  - will on that day cover the entire earth
  *  **from one end of the earth to the other**  - Merism is used to describe two extremes and everything in between. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **They will be like dung on the ground **- This is to refer to the total lack of care for the bodies of the dead. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])

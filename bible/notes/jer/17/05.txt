===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like a cypress tree**  - Yahweh compares man to a cypress tree that is a type of plant that is naked or bare and does not produce fruit or seed. (See: Simile and Translate Unknowns)
  * **inhabitants**  - people that live in a certain place

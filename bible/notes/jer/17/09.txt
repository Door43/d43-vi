===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who can understand it? **- Yahweh knows only he can understand the thoughts of people. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **deserves **- this word is used to say that someone should or should not get something
  * **A partridge hatches an egg that she did not lay - **  The point of the comparison is that the unjustly rich gather their wealth but they do not get to see the fruits of it.
  * **partridge**  - a brown bird with a round body and short tail that is often hunted for food and sport (See: Translate Unknowns)
  * **riches will abandon him - **  AT**: "**he will loose his wealth" (See: Personification)
  * **the end**  - the end of his life

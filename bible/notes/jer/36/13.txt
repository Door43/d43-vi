===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** Micaiah, Jehudi, Nethaniah, Shelemiah, Cushi, Baruch ** - These are the names of men.  (See: Translate Names)
  * **in your hand **  - This phrase is not a figure of speech here, but is written where it is not needed, to be especially clear that Baruch is to take the scroll himself and not send it along with someone else.
  * **the scroll from which yo****u were reading in the people's hearing **  - This phrase gives information about the scroll to be especially clear which scroll Baruch is to take.
  * **in his hand**  - This phrase shows that Baruch did, in fact, take the scroll himself.

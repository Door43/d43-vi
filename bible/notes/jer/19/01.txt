===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Valley of Ben Hinnom**  - See how you translated in 7:31 (See: Translate Names)
  * **Potsherd Gate**  - "Gate of Broken Pots" (UDB) or "East Gate" according to some translations. (See: Translate Names)
  * **the ears of everyone who hears of it will tingle **- Those who hear of the coming disaster will be stunned (UDB), upset, or shocked. AT: "the ears of everyone who hears of it will ring"

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **rescue you from his hand** - AT: "Yahweh will rescue you from the control of the king of Babylon" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to save you and rescue you**  - The words "save" and "rescue" mean the same thing and are repeated for emphasis. AT: "to save you" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])

===== Translation Notes: =====
God continues to speak from verse 7.

  * **Set your mind on the right path, the way you should take.** -  AT: "Try hard to remember the way you came when you were taken into captivity."(See [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Come back, virgin Israel!**  - God is referring to a changed Israel. (See [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **How long will you continue to waver, faithless daughter?** - God is asking how long His people will disobey Him.  (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) AT: "Do not hesitate to start obeying me"

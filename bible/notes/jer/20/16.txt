===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that man**  - This phrase refers to the man who informed Jeremiah's father of Jeremiah's birth. See 20:15
  * **May he hear a call for help in the dawn and the shout of battle at noontime**  - Both "a call for help" and "the shout of battle" are things that are associated with war.  AT: "May he experience the terror of war" (See: Metonymy)
  * **in the dawn**  - "at sunrise"
  * **Why is it that I came out from the womb to see troubles and agony so that my days are filled with shame? - **  Jeremiah complains to God that he was born only to experience shame and difficulty. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **troubles and agony**  - Both terms refer to the extreme difficulties that Jeremiah suffered. (See: Doublet)
  * **agony**  - extreme mental or physical pain

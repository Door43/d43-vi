===== Translation Notes: =====

Jeremiah is now speaking to the people of Edom.

  * **I**  - This "I" in verse 14 refers to Jeremiah.
  * **her**- This "her" refers to Edom.
  * **I**  - This "I" in verse 15 refers to Yahweh.
  * **you**  - This "you" in verse 15 refers to the nation of Edom.

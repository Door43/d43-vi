===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking about what will happen to Kedar.

  * **holes in the ground**
 - "hidden" (See:Metonymy)
  * **Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in 1:8.
  * ** devised a plan**- "made a plan".
  * **Vs 31**  - Yahweh is now speaking to Nebuchadnezzar.
  * **them**
 - "The towns of the nation".

===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh tells what will happen to the people of Damascus.

  * **Arpad**
- This is the name of a place. (See: Translate Names)

  * **They melt away**
- AT: "They are scared!" (See: Metaphor)

  * **They become as troubled as the sea**  - (See: Simile)
  * **Distress seizes it, pain like a woman giving birth**
- (See: Simile)

  * **How is the famous city… **  Yahweh is surprised that the famous city, over which he rejoiced, still has people in it. (See: Rhetorical Question)

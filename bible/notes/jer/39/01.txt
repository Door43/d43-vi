===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the ninth year and tenth month Zedekiah king of Judah** - "When Zedekiah had been king of Judah for 9 years and 10 months" ( See:  [[:vi:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear|biblical time: year]] and [[:vi:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth|biblical time: month]])
  * **In the eleventh year and fourth month of Zedekiah, on the ninth day of the month** - "When Zedekiah had been king for 11 years, 4 months, and 9 days" (See: [[:vi:obe:other:biblicaltimeday|biblical time: day]])
  * **Nergal Sharezer, Samgar Nebo, and Sarsechim** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the middle gate** - "in the middle entrance to the city." It was common for leaders to sit at the gate of the city to discuss important matters.

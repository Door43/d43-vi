===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **It is by the covenant faithfulness of Yahweh that we are not finished off** - AT: "It is by the covenant faithfulness of Yahweh that we have not been not completely destroyed" or "It is because of his covenant faithfulness that Yahweh has not completely destroyed us" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **your faithfulness!** - The writer speaks to Yahweh.
    * **Yahweh is my portion** - "Yahweh himself is what I will receive from him" or "Because Yahweh is with me, I have everything I need"
    * **declares my life** - AT: "I declare" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Let us lift up our heart with our hands...and pray** - AT: "Let us pray sincerely with lifted hands" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **We have transgressed and have rebelled** - The words "transgressed" and "rebelled" share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **against you** - Here the word "you" refers to God.
    * **You have covered yourself with anger** - Possible meanings are 1) "You have hidden yourself from us because you were angry" or 2) "You have surrounded us with your anger." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

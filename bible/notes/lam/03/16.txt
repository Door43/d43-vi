===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my splendor has perished** - Possible meanings are 1) "my prosperity is gone" or 2) "my endurance has come to an end"
  * **so my hope must come from Yahweh** - Possible meanings are 1) "I no longer expect to receive anything good from Yahweh" (UDB) or 2) "I no longer expect to receive anything good, unless it comes from Yahweh."

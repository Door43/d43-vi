===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **the Alpha and the Omega, the Beginning and the End** - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined to emphasize that God is eternal. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]]).
    * **the Alpha and the Omega** - See how you translated this in [[vi:bible:notes:rev:01:07|1:8]].
    * **To the one who thirsts...water of life** - This means God will freely give eternal life to any person that really wants it. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The lips of the wise scatter knowledge about** - AT: "Wise men spread knowledge everywhere by what they say" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **not so the hearts of fools** - AT: "but the hearts of fools do not scatter knowledge about" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **the hearts of fools** - AT: "fools" or "foolish people" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **Yahweh hates the sacrifices of the wicked** - AT: "Yahweh hates it when wicked people sacrifice to him" or "Yahweh hates it when the wicked worship him"
    * **the upright** - See how you translated this word in [[:vi:bible:notes:pro:03:31|3:32]].
    * **is his delight** - AT: "pleases him" or "is Yahweh's delight" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])

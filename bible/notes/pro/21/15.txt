===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When justice is done** - AT: "When rulers do what is just" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **wanders from** - AT: "walks away from" or "abandons"
  * **the way of understanding** - AT: "the wise and just way of living" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he will rest in the assembly of the dead** - "he will remain in the assembly of dead spirits" AT: "he will rest in the place where dead spirits come together to rest"

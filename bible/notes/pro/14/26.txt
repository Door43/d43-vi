===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **his children** - Those are the children of the person who fears Yahweh (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
    * **fountain of life** - AT: "source of life"
    * **that one** - "that a person"
    * **snare** - a sort of trap used to hunt animals

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the hope that was in his strength** - "the confidence he has in his own power"
  * **comes to nothing** - "disappears"
  * **The one who does what is right is kept away from trouble** - "God keeps away from trouble the one who does what is right." (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **it comes** - "trouble comes"

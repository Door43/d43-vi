===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **crucible** - a container used for heating substances to very high temperatures
  * **Though you should crush the fool ... yet his foolishness will not leave him** - This means that even if a fool was crushed like grain is crushed by a mortar and pestle, the foolishness could not be removed from him. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Though you should** - AT: "Even if you"
  * **pestle** - a tool for grinding materials in a mortar

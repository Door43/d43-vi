===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his mouth** - AT: "what he says" (UDB) (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **opens wide his lips** - AT: "speaks a lot" or "talks too much" (UDB) (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **appetite** - See how you translated this in [[:vi:bible:notes:pro:13:01|13:2]].
  * **craves** - "strongly desires"
  * **the appetite of the diligent will be richly satisfied** - AT: "the diligent will have a richly satisfied life" or "being diligent will make you richly satisfied" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the diligent** - people who work with careful and continued effort

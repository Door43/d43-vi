===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **listening ear**  - "person who is willing to listen" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **Speaking a well chosen word, is like apples of gold ...** - These two things are being compared because they both are pleasant. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **apples of gold** - "golden apples"
    * **Like a gold ring or jewelry ... is a wise rebuke ...** - These two things are being compared because they are both cherished and priceless. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])

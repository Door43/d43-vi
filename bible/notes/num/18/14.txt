\v 14 Every devoted thing in Israel will be yours.
\v 15 Everything that opens the womb, all the firstborn which the people offer to Yahweh, both of man and animal, will  be yours. Nevertheless, the people must certainly buy back every firstborn son, and they must buy back the firstborn male of unclean animals.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** Everything that opens the womb ** - This means the firstborn of every human or animal.
  * ** buy back ** - This means to redeem. (See: redeem, redemption)
  * ** bought back ** - This is the past tense meaning to redeem. (See: redeem, redemption )
  * **shekel**  - (See: [[:vi:obe:other:biblicalmoney|biblical money]], [[:vi:obe:other:biblicalweight|biblical weight]])

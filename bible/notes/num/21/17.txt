===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Spring up, well. **- AT: "Let the well fill up with water" (See: Metonymy)
  * **The well that our leaders dug, the well the people's nobles dug**  - wells dug by the leaders or nobles or by those they commanded to dig for them. (See: parallelism)
  * **Mattanah **  - This is the name of a place. (See: Translate names)

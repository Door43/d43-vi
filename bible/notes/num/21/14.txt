===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** the scroll of the Wars of Yahweh ** - Perhaps an ancient poem or collection of songs recording some of Israel's battles
  * **Waheb in Suphah**  - These are both names of places. (See: Translate names)
  * **Arnon**  - This is the name of a river in the valley.
  * **Ar**  - This is the name of the town.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Heshbon **- This is the name of a city. (See: Translate Names)
  * ** Let the city of Sihon be rebuilt and established again  ** - AT: " Rebuild and establish the city of Sihon again" (See: Passive)
  * **A fire blazed from Heshbon, a flame from the city of Sihon**  - (See: parallelism)
  * **fire blazed**  - "fire burned brightly"
  * **Ar**  - This is the name of a city. (See: Translate names)
  * **Arnon**  - This is the name of the river. (See: Translate names)

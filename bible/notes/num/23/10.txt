===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who can count the dust of Jacob or number even only one fourth of Israel?**  - AT: "No one can count the Israelites. No one could count even a fourth of them because there are so many" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]] and [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **and let my life's end be like his**  - Here "his" refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **God brings him** - "God brings the Israelites"
  * **He has strength like a wild ox** - This emphasizes that the Israelites have great strength. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **He will eat up the nations** - Balaam compares the Israelites to a wild animal. This means they will destroy their enemies. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

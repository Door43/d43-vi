===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **does anything in defiance**  - AT: "knowingly breaks my commandments"
  * **That person must be cut off**  - AT: "You must permanently taken away that person" (See: [[:vi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **despised my word **- This means to hate Yahweh's word.
  * **broken my commandment **- This means to go against God's commandment.
  * **His sin will be on him**  - This means his sin will be blamed on him or counted against him. (See: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Those who died by the plague**  - "Those" refers to the people of Israel who died from the plague.
  * **those who had died in the matter of Korah**  - "Those" refers to the people who rebelled with Korah and their families.
  * **in the matter of Korah **  - AT: "with Korah". (See: UDB)

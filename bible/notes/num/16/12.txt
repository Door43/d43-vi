===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Is it a small thing that you have brought us up out of a land flowing with milk and honey, to kill us in the wilderness **- Dathan and Abiram asked this question to rebuke Moses.  AT: "It was not a small thing that you brought us out of a good land to kill us in the wilderness."  (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **a land flowing with milk and honey**  - This means that the land will be rich and productive, so there will be enough food for everyone. AT: “a land that is excellent for cattle and farming” (See: [[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol1/translate/figs_idiom|Idiom]]) (See: Exodus 03: 7-8)
  * **blind us with empty promises **- This means to try and deceive them with false promises. (See: Metaphor)

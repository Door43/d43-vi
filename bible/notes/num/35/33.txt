===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **pollute **  - AT: " defile"
  * **No atonement can be made for the land when blood has been shed on it **  - AT: "Nothing can clean the land when blood from a murder spills on it " (See Passive)
  * **blood has been shed **  - This term literally means “shedding of blood,” which refers to when blood comes out of a person's body from an open wound.

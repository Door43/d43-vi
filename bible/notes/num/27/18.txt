===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Take Joshua son of Nun, a man in whom my Spirit lives**  - Yahweh's Spirit is in Joshua, meaning that Joshua obeys Yahweh and follows his commands.
  * **and command him before their eyes to lead them**  - "Him" refers to Joshua, "their" refers to the people of Israel, and "them" refers to the people of Israel.

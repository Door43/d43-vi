===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a man who may go out and come in before them and lead them out and bring them in**  - AT: "Appoint someone who will lead your people when they go to fight a battle" (UDB)
  * **so that your community is not like sheep that have no shepherd**  - The community is being compared to a group of sheep without a leader, or shepherd.  AT:  "so that your people have a leader and do not wander."  (See: Similie)

===== Translation Notes: =====

Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **an east wind will come, the wind of Yahweh** - This means that the judgment of Yahweh on Israel (Ephraim) is coming soon. (see: [[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Ephraim's spring will dry up, and his well will have no water** - AT: "The people of Israel will meet disaster." (UDB)

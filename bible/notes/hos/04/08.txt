===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking about Israel.

  * **They feed on the sin of my people**  - This refers to the fact that the priests were allowed to eat portions of the sin offerings that the people brought to Yahweh. (see: metaphor and synecdoche)
  * **they are greedy for more of their wickedness**  - //they are greedy for more of their wickedness//  can be made explicit: "So you want the people to sin more and more." (UDB) (see: Explicit and implicit information)
  * **It will be like people, like priests.**  - //It will be like people, like priests//  can be made explicit: "I will punish you priests just as I punish the people." (UDB) (see: Explicit and implicit information)

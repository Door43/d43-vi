===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking about Israel.

  * **lawsuit**  - a complaint by one person against another in a court of law
  * **do not let anyone accuse anyone else**  - //do not let anyone accuse anyone else //  can be made explicit: "You must not allow anyone to correct another person; no one has that right, because everyone is guilty." (UDB) (see: Explicit and implicit information)
  * **I will destroy your mother**  - In this verse, "mother" refers to the nation of Israel. (see: __2:2)__

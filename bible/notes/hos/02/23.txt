===== Translation Notes: =====

God is speaking to Hosea about what he will do for Israel.

  * **I will plant her for myself in the land**  - //I will plant her for myself in the land//  can be made explicit: "At that time, I will take care of the Israelite people as a farmer plants his own land and takes care of his crops." (UDB) (see: Explicit and implicit information)
  * **Lo Ruhamah**  - The name itself means 'not loved,' 'not having obtained mercy', 'without compassion.'
  * **I will say to those who were not my people **- "Lo Ammi" refers to those who were not Yahweh's people
  * **'You are my people' **- "Ammi Attah"

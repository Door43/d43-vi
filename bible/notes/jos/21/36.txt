===== Translation Notes =====

    * **four cities**  - Refers to the list as a sum total. (See: [[vi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **Bezer, Jahaz, Kedemoth, Mephaath,  and Ramoth**  - Names of cities. (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Mahanaim**  - Translate the same way as in Genesis 32:2; Joshua 13:26,30; 2 Samuel 2:8,12,29; 17:24,27; 19:32; 1 Kings 2:8,4:14; 1 Chronicles 6:80 and Song of Solomon 6:13 (See: [[vi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

==== How to Get Answers ====

There are several resources available for finding answers to questions: 

  * **[[vi:ta|translationAcademy]]** - This training manual has much information including:
    * **[[:vi:ta:vol1:translate:toc_transvol1_2|Translation Manual]]** - explains the basics of translation theory and practical translation helps
    * **[[:vi:ta:vol1:checking:toc_checkvol1_2|Checking Manual]]** - explains the basics of checking theory and best practices
    * **[[:vi:ta:vol1:tech:toc_techvol1_2|Technology Manual]]** - explains how to use the different technology tools
    * **[[:vi:ta:vol1:process:toc_processvol1_2|Process Manual]]** - answers the question, "what next?"
  * **[[:en:slack|Slack Chatroom]]** - Join the unfoldingWord community, post your questions to the "helpdesk" channel, and get real-time answers to your questions
  * **Helpdesk** - email help@door43.org with your questions
